# RESEARCH SCOPE — making the Tier-5 (Mundicide) charge measurable — 2026-07-02

*Companion to the assembled Tier-5 body (`docs/ai-energy-climate-pattern-2026-06-30.md`, `docs/ai-compute-footprint-verified-brief-2026-06-30.md`, `cases/xai-boxtown-turbines/`, Ledger Cluster 9 / Entry 13) and to the apex index. The Tier-5 body is **documented conduct with a bounded verdict**; what it has not had is the parallel of the Tier-6 baseline-erasure scope — an explicit statement of which parts of the charge are **quantifiable with pre-committed criteria**, and which are held out of reach by category. This document supplies that, so Tier 5 sits at parity with Tier 6's measurability treatment. It is a **measurability scope (step-zero)**: no data analysed, no account-level guilt asserted (that is step-two, for a tribunal).*

*Custody: builds on the two AI-energy briefs (web-retrieved 2026-06-30, X-1 disciplined) plus named public data infrastructure; this scope is LOCATOR-VERIFIED.*

## The single most important line in this document

**The net climate effect of AI compute is genuinely uncertain, and this scope does not try to make it certain.** The AI-energy brief's own controls establish that (record carbon-free procurement, a regulator saying no, ~10× per-query efficiency gains, the IEA's unrealized-but-real emission-reduction potential). So the measurable Tier-5 charge is **not** "AI is killing the climate." It is the narrower, bounded pair the brief already isolated:

> *(1) fossil-capacity life-extension and new-build attributable to compute load, at the moment and in the sector where the emissions curve was bending; and (2) the asymmetry between an operator's salvation-framing and the fossil electrons actually supplying its load.*

Both are quantifiable. The civilizational-total ("extraction-as-killing") reading is held out of reach on purpose — it is the Tier-5 analogue of the Tier-6 functional-web layer, and any output that lets the measurable pair stand in for the civilizational total is committing the over-reach the brief's controls exist to police.

**But the reverse over-reach is the more dangerous one here, and this scope was initially guilty of it.** There is a *third* object, and it is neither uncertain nor bounded by any emissions control:

> *(3) the **self-authorization** — a small elite arrogating the standing to decide that other people's worlds (present populations, future generations, and the more-than-human worlds that never got a vote) are acceptable stakes in a wager placed on a short compute-scaling horizon, reversing a hard-won climate correction to place it, and narrating the wager as human thriving.*

Object (3) is the hubris. It does **not** depend on the megatonnage, the datacenter share, or whether FERC said no once — those bound Object (1), the accounting, and the accounting was never where the crime lived. Routing the whole charge through (1) and holding (3) out of reach as "account-level guilt / step-two" is itself a laundering move — **empirical hostage** (Tier-4 move 8): making the moral charge hostage to a contested physical quantity, then bounding the quantity, then letting the bound stand in for the charge. The crumb (1.4%) shown so the cake (the self-appointed wager) is not seen. This scope carries Object (3) as a first-class, separately-evidenced leg (leg (d) below), explicitly **untouched by the emissions uncertainty**, precisely so the discipline that bounds the accounting cannot be turned into a shield for the actors. Over-applied epistemic humility toward power is deference to power; see `docs/external-review-2026-07-01-sycophancy-to-power-self-audit.md`.

## The claim under test (falsifiable form, pre-committed)

> *In specific grids/utilities, (a) fossil generation capacity that would otherwise have retired has had its retirement postponed, or new fossil capacity has been built, with datacenter/AI load named as a driver in the operator's own regulatory filings; (b) the grid's CO₂-intensity trend reverses or its decline flattens in a step time-locked to datacenter load growth, beyond what gas prices and weather explain; and (c) the operator's public salvation-framing (clean-energy PPAs, net-zero pledges) is disproportionate to its disclosed fossil-supplied load.*

**How it loses:** if filings attribute postponements to other drivers (reliability, gas price, general demand) with datacenter load absent; if grid-intensity trend breaks track gas/weather rather than compute load; if disclosed clean supply actually covers the marginal load (framing matched by electrons); or if the postponed capacity would have retired on the same schedule anyway (no counterfactual gap). Any of these refutes the corresponding leg — and the legs are scored independently, so partial results are honest partial results.

## Nulls / failure modes — marked to raise veracity, not lower it

- **N-1 — Attribution laundering (undisclosed drivers).** Filings may attribute a postponement to "reliability" or "load growth" generically while datacenter load is the true marginal cause. The audit can only count *self-attributed* cases; the undisclosed remainder is a floor, not a ceiling, and must be labelled as such (it under-counts, never over-counts).
- **N-2 — Gas-price and weather confounds.** Coal/gas dispatch and retirement economics move with fuel prices and demand shocks independent of compute. Leg (b) is only interpretable with these controlled; uncontrolled, a trend break proves nothing.
- **N-3 — Efficiency offset / Jevons ambiguity.** Per-query energy fell ~10× (brief C-3); absolute load still rose. The scope measures *absolute* attributable fossil MWh/Mt, not per-unit efficiency — and must state that a per-unit improvement does not net out an absolute increase, nor vice-versa. Direction of the *absolute* series is the estimand.
- **N-4 — The counterfactual problem.** "Would have retired anyway" is the load-bearing counterfactual for leg (a). Postponement must be measured against the *operator's own prior published retirement date*, not an analyst's guess; plants already uneconomic for other reasons are excluded or flagged.
- **N-5 — The modest-share control (the crumb/cake).** Datacenter emissions are a small % of the total (brief's own boundary). The scope must **not** inflate the share; the charge is the *marginal directional reversal at the bending point*, and the index reports the share honestly beside the directional finding so the crumb is never passed off as the cake — nor the cake dismissed as the crumb.
- **N-6 — Framing asymmetry ≠ deception.** Leg (c) measures a gap, not a lie. A large gap is consistent with genuine-but-lagging transition. The index is side-neutral and stops at asymmetry; converting asymmetry to a deception finding is step-two, on a fuller record.
- **N-7 — Self-authorization is a structure, not a sincerity finding (leg (d)).** Leg (d) does not claim the principals are lying or malicious; it may all be sincerely believed (the ledger already concedes Altman's may be genuine belief). The charge is the *structure* — self-appointed standing + harms unspecified at population level + no falsifiable stopping condition + wagered worlds unrepresented — which is damning *whether or not* it is sincere, because sincerity in the wagerer does not confer consent from the wagered. Conversely: the *acceptable-casualty magnitude* (the "300M–1B" figure) must be sourced to specific statements in the corpus, not asserted — grounding or bounding that number is leg (d)'s own work, per X-1 discipline. The structure does not need the number; the number, if grounded, sharpens it.

## Conversion criteria — what the scope decides, pre-committed

- **→ MEASURABLE / SUPPORTED (per leg, scored independently):** (a) a non-trivial counted inventory of self-attributed fossil life-extension vs. prior schedule; (b) a grid-intensity trend break time-locked to compute load surviving gas/weather controls; (c) a framing-vs-electrons asymmetry beyond disclosed clean coverage; **(d) a documented self-authorization structure — inevitability + aggregate-benefit framing, harms unspecified at population level, no falsifiable stopping condition, wagered worlds unrepresented — read off the principals' own captured statements.** The Tier-5 charge is then quantified/documented, not merely asserted. **Leg (d) is the primary charge and can succeed even if (a)–(c) are equivocal**, because the hubris does not require the accounting.
- **→ MEASURABLE / REFUTED (per leg):** (a) filings absent datacenter attribution; (b) trend breaks tracking gas/weather; (c) framing matched by electrons; **(d) principals *do* specify harms at the population level and *do* state a falsifiable stopping condition and *do* seat the affected in the decision** — i.e. the authorization structure is absent. Recorded as tested-and-unsupported for that leg. (Leg (d)'s refutation condition is stringent and public; that it is so rarely met is itself the finding.)
- **Held OUT OF REACH in all cases:** the *net civilizational climate effect* of AI (genuinely uncertain per the brief's C-4), the *subjective sincerity* of any principal (leg (d) is structure, not mind-reading — N-7), and a *tribunal verdict* of account-level guilt (step-two). **What is NOT out of reach — and this is the correction:** the self-authorization *structure itself* (leg (d)) is step-one evidence-bearing and is the primary charge. The earlier draft mislabeled the whole hubris as "account-level guilt / step-two" and thereby held the actual crime out of reach behind the accounting's uncertainty — the empirical-hostage move named in the top-line boundary. The category boundary is around the *verdict* and the *net-effect physics*, never around the documented wager.

## Scope boundary

**Establishes:** that the bounded Tier-5 charge is **quantifiable** through named public data infrastructure (regulatory filings, EPA/EIA grid data, disclosure corpora), with per-leg pre-committed support/refute criteria — so "verified conduct pattern + salvation-frame corpus" is a *measurable* evidence mode, not a rhetorical one.

**Does NOT establish:** any result (no data analysed here); the net climate effect; account-level guilt; or that framing asymmetry is deception. It commissions nothing and asserts no tribunal-facing claim.

**Feasibility note (honest):** the cheapest *and* primary first result is **leg (d)** — the self-authorization corpus — because much of it is **already captured in this repo**: Altman's "gentle singularity" entry already carries the exact finding leg (d) scores (aggregate-positive framing, harms absorbed into the aggregate before population-level specification, no falsifiable stopping condition), and Ledger Cluster 6 / Entry 13 carry the p(doom) and longtermist acceptable-cost material. Formalizing these into a scored corpus against the Entry-13 circuit-breaker test is a documentary pass with existing custody and no contested physics. Leg **(a)** (the self-attribution fossil-audit) is the cheapest *accounting* result and remains the natural second pre-registration (the Tier-5 analogue of `experiments/paleo-baseline-erasure/`). Ordering matters: **(d) first, because the hubris is the charge and it is the most provable, not the least.**
