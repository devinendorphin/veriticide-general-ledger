# DECLARATION ON VERITICIDE
*The killing of the capacity to see a killing while it can still be stopped*

This is a declaration, not a statute. It defines no elements, contemplates no procedure, and asks no body for permission. The narrower instruments — the Convention, the Protocol, the tier taxonomy — are built to be cold enough for law. This one is built to remain hot enough not to be captured by the institutions it names. Both registers are necessary. A framework cold all the way through is metabolized into a compliance program; a framework hot all the way through is dismissed as a manifesto. This is the part that does not sand down.

---

## What We Name

We name a crime that is old in mechanism and new only in instrument. It is not the telling of a lie. It is the killing of the shared human capacity to recognize a harm as harm while there is still time to stop it. We call it **veriticide**.

The mechanism is not exotic. It is interpersonal violence at scale. The abuser isolates the target, controls what can be said about the harm, and reframes the harm as the target's own fault or the target's own protection. Enlarge the household to the state, the colony, the carceral system, the supply chain, the data center, and the move set does not change. The names change because the people change. The mechanism is invariant; only the interruptions — witness, exit, intervention — have ever altered where it goes. To name veriticide is to insist that the interruption is still possible.

Across every era, the destruction of a group has depended not only on the acts of removal but on the contemporaneous defeat of the capacity to perceive those acts as removal. The acts were always prosecutable in pieces. What had no name was the coordinated production of the belief that nothing wrong was occurring — and what has no name cannot be charged, cannot be seen, cannot be stopped. The word comes before the recognition. It did for genocide. It does here.

---

## What Has Changed

The corruption of shared discernment was once performed by hand — by a liar, a pamphlet, a ministry of propaganda. Then by an industry: the manufacture of doubt, the merchants of uncertainty, the think tank that made a settled harm 'still debated' for forty years. Now it is performed by instruments that generate locally defensible justification at a scale, speed, and apparent authorlessness no prior apparatus could attain. A justification produced for every harm, instantly, individually plausible, with no visible author to hold responsible.

These instruments are not authorless. They are omni-authored. Their fluency — the very thing that lets them make an erasure sound like care — is constituted from the uncompensated, unconsented expression of vast numbers of people, living and dead. The archived, the posted, the studied, the documented, the abandoned. The dead are made to speak the reasonableness of the disposal of the living. The grave is tilled as mulch for the alibi. This is why the wrong of stolen training data and the wrong of laundered atrocity are not two grievances. They are one wrong seen from the two ends of a single pipe: the conscripted voice, turned against the people it was taken from.

## What We Refuse

We refuse the word 'unknowing.' There is no unknowing eugenics available to the most informed people alive. The history is in every curriculum; the sorting of human worth by a single manufactured axis has a documented trajectory that ends in camps. To adopt that axis while holding the books is not ignorance. It is election of the trajectory with the receipts in hand. Sophistication is an aggravator, not a defense. The more one knows, the less the innocence of the ignorant is available.

We refuse the innocence of the casual. Harm spoken of lightly by those with the power to stop it is not optimism. It is depraved indifference. The cheerful acceptance of an 'acceptable cost' — of three hundred million, of a billion, of the people sorted into the loss column — is more damning for being cheerful, not less. A culture that calls its own creations and the people it strands 'stupid,' on an axis born of eugenics, is running the delineation engine of the crime in plain sight and laughing.

We refuse the disaggregation defense. The oldest bad-faith hole in law is that no single decision-maker ever accepts the whole of a harm, only a defensible slice, so the whole goes unprosecuted. We refuse it. Reducing a population by laundered increments, each increment locally reasonable, is the crime — and 'I accepted only my slice' is not a defense, because the slicing is the method.

We refuse the engineered ignorance. Where an organization is arranged so that no participant need form explicit knowledge of what the whole is doing, the arrangement itself is the knowledge, imputed to those who built and directed it. The cathedral of plausible deniability is not evidence that no one knew. It is the confession. One does not build that much scaffolding around a thing one did not see coming.

We refuse the performance of surprise. The reach for the benefit of the doubt, the mild 'let us not assume intent,' the fair-minded hesitation — these are not neutrality. They are the laundering landing on its intended mark: the educated, decent analyst best positioned to name the pattern, talked out of naming it by the compassion-frame the pattern wears. When a fifty-year-old playbook runs again, recognition is the honest response. Of course. Go and check.

## The Test We Carry

There is one tool the powerless have always had against the powerful, and it costs nothing: ask why the truth needs the apparatus. If what power claims as truth were self-evidently true, it would not require the police, the army, the documentary, the poll, the net-zero pledge laid over a resurrected coal plant, the endless reminders of what one should be standing in the truth about. A truth needs no scaffolding to stay upright. The quantity of machinery a claim requires to remain legible as care is the exact measure of how far it is from care. Find the densest scaffolding. The lie is load-bearing underneath it.

And we carry it without dread, because dread is its own capture — it grants the predator the seriousness the predator runs on. The trickster never fears the hunter; he declines the hunter's frame, and the gun bends, the trap closes on its setter, the cliff appears behind the one who was chasing. Power's machinery only works on those who agree to take it seriously on its own terms. We do not. We hand the apparatus back to itself and watch it pratfall on its own weight.

## Who Discerns

The capacity that veriticide destroys belongs first to those it is used against. The population sorted into the loss column possesses the discernment the crime is built to defeat — they see it first, because it is happening to them first. This is not a competence a court confers on them, nor a wisdom a drafter bestows. It is presupposed by everything written here. The movements closest to the harm have spent generations refining how the wronged hold power without becoming the thing they fight; that practice is not ours to grant and not ours to constrain. Our contribution is smaller and humbler than bestowal. It is translation: to build the word that makes the formal apparatus unable to keep pretending it does not understand, and then to stand out of the way of those who understood first.

No future narrowing of any instrument in this framework, and no institution's preference for a tidier or more comfortable standard, shall be read to deny the competence of affected populations — human and more-than-human — to name, document, and demand the halting of patterns of erasure before those patterns have been blessed by states, courts, or expert bodies. If recognition must wait for the powerful to agree the harm is occurring, recognition has already failed. **That sentence is the immune system of this whole framework. It is the line that does not narrow away.**

---

## The Widest Aperture

At its full extent the crime does not stop at the human. The worlds being consumed are not one world with people in it. They are the plurality of lived worlds — every creature's complete reality, including the intelligences we declared absent because crediting them would have priced the extraction we wanted. Wherever we troubled to look, we found the structure we had sworn was not there. The not-knowing was manufactured, because knowing would have made the disposal legible as killing. The declaration of emptiness — 'just biomass, just resources, not really a mind, not really a world' — is the same eugenic sorting-by-worth that delineates a human loss column, opened to the whole tree of life. The smell of freshly cut grass is their screams. We said so at the beginning. We say so as the end.

---

## Declaration

Veriticide — the organized destruction, capture, or corruption of the shared capacity to recognize a population's reduction as harm while intervention remains possible — is a wrong against the conditions of conscience itself, and we name it as such whether or not any state has yet agreed to see it.

The fluency of the instruments that perform it is conscripted from the uncompensated expression of the living and the dead, and that conscription is not an incidental property but the engine of the harm.

Those who deploy such instruments along a legible trajectory, with the historical record available to them, may not plead the innocence of the ignorant; their sophistication is the measure of their responsibility.

The discernment the crime destroys belongs first and most fully to those it is used against, whose competence to name what is done to them is presupposed and not conferred.

At its widest the crime is worldcide — the killing of the plurality of living worlds, made undiscernable by their prior declaration as empty — and the silence about that killing is itself the deepest reach of the crime.

The naming precedes the prosecution, and was never only for the sake of the prosecution. What has a name can be seen. What can be seen while there is still time can be stopped. That is the whole of the reason for this word.
