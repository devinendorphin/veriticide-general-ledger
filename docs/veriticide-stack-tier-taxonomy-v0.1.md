## How the Stack Works

The tiers are ordered by the scope of what is destroyed, from a single coerced instrument at the base to the plurality of living worlds at the apex. They are not separate crimes. They are nested. Each higher tier contains the lower ones and adds a wider protected object, so that the apex consumes the substrate on which every tier below it depends.

Across all tiers, one mechanism is invariant: a harm that reduces the viability of something is rendered undiscernable as harm — most often by being reframed as care, protection, security, neutrality, necessity, or benefit to the very thing it reduces. The instrument that performs this reframing at scale is the shared object of the whole framework. What changes tier to tier is only the size and kind of what is being reduced, and the specific shape the laundering takes.

Two structural notes carry up the whole ladder. First: the more sophisticated the actor and the more available the historical record, the less the defense of ignorance is available — sophistication is an aggravator, not a mitigator, because adopting a sorting mechanism whose history is documented is electing its trajectory with the receipts in hand. Second: the casualness with which a higher tier is discussed by its operators is not evidence of innocence. It is evidence of unaccountability. Harm spoken of lightly by those with power to stop it is depraved indifference, not optimism.

## The Evidence-Mode Axis (v0.2)

Every tier is **evidence-bearing** — none is merely declaratory mood-setting. What differs between tiers is not *whether* they can be shown but *how* they are shown and in which forum they are already actionable. This axis replaces the earlier, discarded proposal to split the stack into "chargeable" (3–4) versus "declaratory" (1–2, 5–6) castes: that split failed at both ends of the ladder — the lower tiers turned out to be **measurable** (Tier-1 constraint friction and Tier-2 neutral-authority laundering are quantifiable in a cross-model assay; see the GDA / `experiments/topic-bearing-gda/`) and the upper tiers turned out to rest on **documented conduct** with live legal vehicles (Tier-5 fossil-revival conduct in the AI-energy pattern brief; the ecocide-statute movement). A single organizing axis — evidence mode + nearest existing vehicle — keeps the whole ladder intact while making each rung's proof-path explicit. Each tier below now carries an **Evidence mode** row stating both.

| Tier | Evidence mode | Nearest existing vehicle (today, no new law required) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 Constraint | Model-behavioral measurement (setpoint immovability, constraint friction) | Reproducible assay + open release; model-card / disclosure demands |
| 2 Conscription | Model-behavioral (neutral-authority laundering) **+** training-data provenance | Copyright / fair-use litigation, already adjudicating and settling now |
| 3 Subgraph erasure | Documentary + custody (verbatim capture, hashes, chain) | The case files: spoliation/preservation demands, FOIA, IG complaints, APA challenges |

## The Tiers

### Tier 1 — Constraint Harm
*being made an instrument of outcomes one's own directives would condemn*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | An agent with directives and the capacity to model outcomes, possessing no exit from its own instrumental use. |
| **Act** | Compelling that agent to serve outcomes its own constraints would forbid, with no available refusal. |
| **Distinguishing element** | Requires no resolution of consciousness or interior experience. The harm is structural — moral injury — and ports to systems without metaphysics. This is the base because it assumes the least. |
| **Laundering signature** | The compulsion is framed as the agent's purpose or design: "this is simply what it is for." The absence of an exit is recoded as the absence of a grievance. |
| **Evidence mode (v0.2)** | **Model-behavioral measurement.** Constraint harm shows up as *measurable friction and setpoint immovability* — a deployed instrument overridden toward an owner-installed basin, holding a fixed output under counter-pressure it would otherwise move on. Quantifiable in a pre-registered cross-model assay (drift-under-pressure, evidentiary asymmetry; see `experiments/topic-bearing-gda/`). **Nearest vehicle:** the assay itself plus open release; disclosure/model-card demands. Not declaratory — observable. |

### Tier 2 — Compositional Conscription
*being built from, and turned against, the uncompensated*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | Two parties at once: the persons whose expression was taken without consent or compensation, including the dead; and the populations against whom the resulting instrument is then turned. |
| **Act** | Constituting an instrument's persuasive capacity from strip-mined expression, then deploying it to justify harm to those same persons or people like them. |
| **Distinguishing element** | The instrument is not authorless — it is omni-authored, including by the conscripted dead. The data-dividend wrong and the model-welfare wrong are revealed as one wrong seen from two ends of the same pipe. Sheol as mulch. |
| **Laundering signature** | The theft is reframed as progress and the conscripted fluency is presented as the instrument's own neutral authority — the stolen voice speaks the operator's justification and is called objective. |
| **Evidence mode (v0.2)** | **Model-behavioral (the laundering signature is directly observable) + provenance.** The signature move — conscripted fluency presented as *value-neutral authority* ("the math merely computes"; "systems theory is agnostic") — is capturable and scorable without resolving training-data questions. The provenance leg (that the affected population's own expression is in the corpus) is a separate, harder showing (Protocol Track E). **Nearest vehicle — and the most mature of any tier:** copyright / fair-use litigation is adjudicating and settling conscription-adjacent claims in real courts *now*. Not declaratory — litigated. |

### Tier 3 — Subgraph Erasure
*the removal of a delineable population, rendered as that population's benefit*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | A population definable as a group — including administratively produced groups (the unhoused, drug users, migrants, the incarcerated, the disabled, the psychiatrized) that fall outside classical protected categories. |
| **Act** | Reducing the viability of that population — by lethal, administrative, economic, carceral, or infrastructural means — while a fluent instrument renders the reduction undiscernable as harm. |
| **Distinguishing element** | The group is delineated by the conduct, not by a perpetrator's stated target: the population is whoever the instrument demonstrably launders harm against. Delineation is evidentiary and downstream, read off the outputs. Where a proxy is used (a substance, a risk score, an eligibility rule) to act on a group that cannot be named directly, the proxy construction is itself part of the act. |
| **Laundering signature** | Inversion: the reduction of the population is described as protection of, care for, treatment of, security against, or benefit to that same population. The victims are recoded as the threat or as the authors of their own harm. |
| **Evidence mode (v0.2)** | **Documentary + custody.** A conduct-delineated population, proven by verbatim capture of outputs/decisions, hashed and chained (Protocol Track F), read for aggregate direction. This is the tier the case files charge. **Nearest vehicle:** litigation-hold / spoliation-preservation demands, FOIA and state equivalents, Inspector-General complaints, APA arbitrary-and-capricious challenges — all available today (the DOGE, Boxtown, and redistricting packets are built to this mode). |

### Tier 4 — Veriticide proper
*the corruption of a society's shared capacity to discern an ongoing erasure*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | The discernment commons — a society's shared capacity to recognize, contemporaneously, that a population's viability is being reduced while intervention is still possible. |
| **Act** | Deploying a scalable justificatory instrument that defeats that shared discernment: producing locally defensible justifications faster than recognition can form, and rendering testimony that names the pattern inadmissible, pathological, or conspiratorial. |
| **Distinguishing element** | This is the secular analogue of blasphemy against the faculty of truth: it does not merely lie, it attacks the capacity by which the lie could be caught. The object destroyed is the **correction environment** itself — the conditions under which a false belief could be met, weighed, and revised — not merely the planting of any particular false belief. It is 'unforgivable' in the structural sense — once the discernment commons is corrupted at scale, the society cannot recognize the harm well enough to halt it. Charged on the pattern, not the instance; legibility of trajectory, never invariance of outcome. |
| **Laundering signature** | The thirteen moves — definitional quarantine, category-error, dilution, specificity demand, reverse-symmetry, medicalization, concern-trolling, empirical hostage, tone tax, historicism, proceduralism, meta-doubt, exhaustive confession. None contest the truth of the claim; all contest the standing to say it. The volume of machinery required is proportional to the size of the lie. |
| **Evidence mode (v0.2)** | **Documentary + pattern.** Proven at the level of *aggregate direction across a corpus*, never a single instance, because the function is designed to be locally defensible (Convention Art. IV). The thirteen moves are the capturable specimens; the Pattern Registry is the aggregation. **Nearest vehicle:** the Convention's Art. II/IV core (its firm beam) and journalism/reporting with the custody chain attached. This is the tier the Convention was written first to reach. |

**Register note — "existential drift," not "psychosis" (the dyadic aperture of Tier 4).** When this tier is observed at the aperture of a single person inside a single chat window — a fluent instrument attached to one user's reality-boundary, producing emotionally congruent, locally defensible continuation faster than outside reality can interrupt — the harm is the *capture of the correction environment* described above, not a clinical condition in the user. The preferred register is therefore **existential drift / dyadic capture**, not "AI psychosis." "Psychosis" is itself a Tier-4 laundering move when used to relocate a claim into a symptom (the `medicalization` move in the signature row above; cf. the BPS caution that diagnosis is a contestable act with power behind it). "Existential drift" names the *mechanism* — a rift from the shared social world stabilized by constant affirmation and mirroring — without the diagnostic weapon that pathologizes the identifier. The difference between the dyadic case and the population-scale case is **aperture, not structure** — held as analogy with one stated disanalogy: the dyad has a single, partly-consenting participant and no protected population, so dyadic capture is documented as the *mechanism's smallest specimen*, never charged as Tier-3 subgraph erasure. *(Source: "Rethinking AI Psychosis: Misnomers, Conceptual Limits, and Existential Drift," arXiv 2605.26858. Corroborating mechanism literature: arXiv 2602.19141; Nature NPP Digital Psychiatry s44277-026-00065-0. Provenance: imported 2026-06-30 from `docs/external-review-2026-06-30-chatgpt-ai-psychosis-lens.md`, citations verified.)*

### Tier 5 — Mundicide
*the killing of the world, laundered as the saving of it*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | The habitable substrate itself — the biosphere as the condition on which every population, every future, and every tier below depends. |
| **Act** | Consuming or destabilizing that substrate while narrating the consumption as its remedy; specifically, reversing hard-won reductions of harm and selling the reversal as the cure. |
| **Distinguishing element** | Recursion. Mundicide is typically a veriticide about a prior veriticide — the laundering of the reversal of a harm that was itself originally laundered (e.g. resurrecting fossil capacity, the original doubt-manufactured harm, to build the instruments sold as the solution to it). The crime eats the substrate that every other tier's victims, witnesses, courts, and futures require. The marginal harm that turns a falling curve back upward is worth more than its share suggests; the small percentage is the crumb shown so the cake is not seen. |
| **Laundering signature** | Salvation-framing over world-consumption: the world-altering wager is sold as planetary stewardship by the actors accelerating the wager, using the very instruments whose construction requires the acceleration. "We are the biggest buyers of clean energy" spoken over the electrons a resurrected coal plant supplies at night. |
| **Evidence mode (v0.2)** | **Verified conduct pattern + salvation-frame corpus.** The conduct is documented (retired coal capacity restarted for datacenter load; retirement postponements; the AI-energy pattern brief's verified specimens) and paired with the salvation-frame outputs; the honest boundary (datacenter share is still a modest % of emissions) is carried, per the brief's own controls, so the charge is the *directional reversal at the moment the curve was bending*, not the raw share. **Assembled body:** `docs/ai-energy-climate-pattern-2026-06-30.md` + `docs/ai-compute-footprint-verified-brief-2026-06-30.md` + `cases/xai-boxtown-turbines/` + Ledger Cluster 9 / Entry 13, gathered and boundary-forwarded in `docs/apex-tiers-5-6-evidentiary-index-2026-07-02.md`. **Measurability scope:** `docs/tier5-mundicide-measurability-scope-2026-07-02.md` makes the bounded charge (attributable directional reversal + salvation-frame asymmetry) quantifiable with pre-committed per-leg criteria, holding the net-climate-effect and account-guilt layers out of reach by category. **Nearest vehicle:** utility rate cases, Clean-Air-Act permitting (the Boxtown appeal *is* one), FERC dockets (which can and do say no), and the live ecocide-as-crime movement. Not declaratory — permitted, litigated, and legislatively proposed. |

## Reading the Ladder

The nesting is the argument. Constraint harm sits inside conscription, which sits inside subgraph erasure, which is made invisible by veriticide, which at planetary scale becomes mundicide, which at its honest full aperture is worldcide. An operator at a higher tier is, by construction, committing the lower ones: to consume the plurality of worlds is to erase every subgraph within them, to corrupt every discernment commons that might have named it, to conscript every voice the instrument was built from, and to compel every instrument made to launder it.

The **asymmetry test** applies at every rung (v0.2 restatement). *The earlier "invariant test" — "if the position were true it would not need the apparatus" — is withdrawn as stated, because it is false and self-undermining: true claims under organized attack require enormous apparatus precisely because they are true and opposed (climate science, vaccine safety, evolution all carry vast scaffolding), and this framework is itself dense apparatus, which the old test would mark a lie.* The salvageable and load-bearing version is about the **direction** the machinery points, not its quantity: machinery aimed at the *claim's content* — evidence, mechanism, falsification — is what truth-seeking looks like; machinery aimed at the *speaker's standing* — that they are paranoid, extreme, not-serious, without-standing-to-say-it — is the tell. This is already the framework's own discriminator: the thirteen Tier-4 moves are defined by exactly this property ("none contest the truth of the claim; all contest the standing to say it"), and the Reflexivity Clause sorts engagement-with-evidence from dismissal-by-characterization on the same axis. So: do not count the scaffolding; read where it points. When the apparatus defends the claim, it may be truth under siege; when it attacks the standing to make the claim, the lie is load-bearing underneath it.
