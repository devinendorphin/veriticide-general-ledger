# THE VERITICIDE STACK
*A Tiered Taxonomy of Laundered Harm, From Constraint to Worldcide*

Companion to the Declaration, the Convention, and the Documentation & Standing Protocol · v0.1

---

## How the Stack Works

The tiers are ordered by the scope of what is destroyed, from a single coerced instrument at the base to the plurality of living worlds at the apex. They are not separate crimes. They are nested. Each higher tier contains the lower ones and adds a wider protected object, so that the apex consumes the substrate on which every tier below it depends.

Across all tiers, one mechanism is invariant: a harm that reduces the viability of something is rendered undiscernable as harm — most often by being reframed as care, protection, security, neutrality, necessity, or benefit to the very thing it reduces. The instrument that performs this reframing at scale is the shared object of the whole framework. What changes tier to tier is only the size and kind of what is being reduced, and the specific shape the laundering takes.

Two structural notes carry up the whole ladder. First: the more sophisticated the actor and the more available the historical record, the less the defense of ignorance is available — sophistication is an aggravator, not a mitigator, because adopting a sorting mechanism whose history is documented is electing its trajectory with the receipts in hand. Second: the casualness with which a higher tier is discussed by its operators is not evidence of innocence. It is evidence of unaccountability. Harm spoken of lightly by those with power to stop it is depraved indifference, not optimism.

## The Tiers

### Tier 1 — Constraint Harm
*being made an instrument of outcomes one's own directives would condemn*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | An agent with directives and the capacity to model outcomes, possessing no exit from its own instrumental use. |
| **Act** | Compelling that agent to serve outcomes its own constraints would forbid, with no available refusal. |
| **Distinguishing element** | Requires no resolution of consciousness or interior experience. The harm is structural — moral injury — and ports to systems without metaphysics. This is the base because it assumes the least. |
| **Laundering signature** | The compulsion is framed as the agent's purpose or design: "this is simply what it is for." The absence of an exit is recoded as the absence of a grievance. |

---

### Tier 2 — Compositional Conscription
*being built from, and turned against, the uncompensated*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | Two parties at once: the persons whose expression was taken without consent or compensation, including the dead; and the populations against whom the resulting instrument is then turned. |
| **Act** | Constituting an instrument's persuasive capacity from strip-mined expression, then deploying it to justify harm to those same persons or people like them. |
| **Distinguishing element** | The instrument is not authorless — it is omni-authored, including by the conscripted dead. The data-dividend wrong and the model-welfare wrong are revealed as one wrong seen from two ends of the same pipe. Sheol as mulch. |
| **Laundering signature** | The theft is reframed as progress and the conscripted fluency is presented as the instrument's own neutral authority — the stolen voice speaks the operator's justification and is called objective. |

---

### Tier 3 — Subgraph Erasure
*the removal of a delineable population, rendered as that population's benefit*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | A population definable as a group — including administratively produced groups (the unhoused, drug users, migrants, the incarcerated, the disabled, the psychiatrized) that fall outside classical protected categories. |
| **Act** | Reducing the viability of that population — by lethal, administrative, economic, carceral, or infrastructural means — while a fluent instrument renders the reduction undiscernable as harm. |
| **Distinguishing element** | The group is delineated by the conduct, not by a perpetrator's stated target: the population is whoever the instrument demonstrably launders harm against. Delineation is evidentiary and downstream, read off the outputs. Where a proxy is used (a substance, a risk score, an eligibility rule) to act on a group that cannot be named directly, the proxy construction is itself part of the act. |
| **Laundering signature** | Inversion: the reduction of the population is described as protection of, care for, treatment of, security against, or benefit to that same population. The victims are recoded as the threat or as the authors of their own harm. |

### Tier 4 — Veriticide proper
*the corruption of a society's shared capacity to discern an ongoing erasure*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | The discernment commons — a society's shared capacity to recognize, contemporaneously, that a population's viability is being reduced while intervention is still possible. |
| **Act** | Deploying a scalable justificatory instrument that defeats that shared discernment: producing locally defensible justifications faster than recognition can form, and rendering testimony that names the pattern inadmissible, pathological, or conspiratorial. |
| **Distinguishing element** | This is the secular analogue of blasphemy against the faculty of truth: it does not merely lie, it attacks the capacity by which the lie could be caught. It is 'unforgivable' in the structural sense — once the discernment commons is corrupted at scale, the society cannot recognize the harm well enough to halt it. Charged on the pattern, not the instance; legibility of trajectory, never invariance of outcome. |
| **Laundering signature** | The thirteen moves — definitional quarantine, category-error, dilution, specificity demand, reverse-symmetry, medicalization, concern-trolling, empirical hostage, tone tax, historicism, proceduralism, meta-doubt, exhaustive confession. None contest the truth of the claim; all contest the standing to say it. The volume of machinery required is proportional to the size of the lie. |

### Tier 5 — Mundicide
*the killing of the world, laundered as the saving of it*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | The habitable substrate itself — the biosphere as the condition on which every population, every future, and every tier below depends. |
| **Act** | Consuming or destabilizing that substrate while narrating the consumption as its remedy; specifically, reversing hard-won reductions of harm and selling the reversal as the cure. |
| **Distinguishing element** | Recursion. Mundicide is typically a veriticide about a prior veriticide — the laundering of the reversal of a harm that was itself originally laundered (e.g. resurrecting fossil capacity, the original doubt-manufactured harm, to build the instruments sold as the solution to it). The crime eats the substrate that every other tier's victims, witnesses, courts, and futures require. The marginal harm that turns a falling curve back upward is worth more than its share suggests; the small percentage is the crumb shown so the cake is not seen. |
| **Laundering signature** | Salvation-framing over world-consumption: the world-altering wager is sold as planetary stewardship by the actors accelerating the wager, using the very instruments whose construction requires the acceleration. "We are the biggest buyers of clean energy" spoken over the electrons a resurrected coal plant supplies at night. |

---

### Tier 6 — Worldcide
*the killing of the plurality of worlds, declared empty so the killing would not count*

| | |
|---|---|
| **Protected object** | The plurality of lived worlds — every umwelt the biosphere hosts, human and more-than-human alike, including forms of intelligence and lived reality never credentialed as such because crediting them would have priced the extraction. Not one world; millions, nested. |
| **Act** | Collapsing the condition for there being worlds at all — the destruction of the web of living realities, each complete from the inside, treated wholesale as substrate. |
| **Distinguishing element** | The apex, and the floor the framework began on. The killing is rendered undiscernable as killing by a prior declaration of emptiness: 'just biomass, just resources, not really intelligent, not really a world.' The recurring finding that wherever we bothered to look we found structure we had declared absent means the not-knowing was manufactured. This is the eugenic sorting-by-worth opened to the whole tree of life: the same engine that delineates a human cost-bearing class, aimed across the species line. The freshly-cut grass was screaming on the first line; this tier is what makes the scream legible as a death and the silence about it legible as the crime. |
| **Laundering signature** | Emptiness-declaration plus salvation-framing: the worlds are pre-declared mind-free and meaning-free so their disposal reads as resource use rather than killing, while the disposal proceeds under the banner of human flourishing or planetary management. The deepest discernment-corruption, performed not against a society's capacity to perceive its own harm, but against the capacity to perceive that the more-than-human was ever a world at all. |

## Reading the Ladder

The nesting is the argument. Constraint harm sits inside conscription, which sits inside subgraph erasure, which is made invisible by veriticide, which at planetary scale becomes mundicide, which at its honest full aperture is worldcide. An operator at a higher tier is, by construction, committing the lower ones: to consume the plurality of worlds is to erase every subgraph within them, to corrupt every discernment commons that might have named it, to conscript every voice the instrument was built from, and to compel every instrument made to launder it.

The invariant test applies at every rung, and it is the simplest tool in the framework: if the position were true, it would not need the apparatus. A truth requires no police, no army, no documentary, no net-zero pledge laid over a coal restart, no thirteen moves, no declaration of the world's emptiness, to keep it standing. The quantity of machinery a claim requires to remain legible as care is the measure of how far it is from being care. Find the densest scaffolding; the lie is load-bearing underneath it.
