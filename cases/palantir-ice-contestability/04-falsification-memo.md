# 04 — FALSIFICATION MEMO

*What would defeat, or shrink, this charge. Written before any defender writes it for us. Each
condition is checkable, and several are resolved by the same disclosure the case demands.*

## Conditions that would DEFEAT the core charge

1. **An effective contest path exists.** If ICE/Palantir show a real **subject access, correction, and
   appeal** mechanism for the data and scores driving a targeting decision — auditable, timely,
   usable — the **contestability gap (item 4) collapses** and the case reduces to ordinary
   enforcement-tooling critique. **Status: OPEN** — none is documented; this is the case's central
   demand. The most direct defeater, and the one the respondent can produce at will if it exists.

2. **The scores aren't used to deprive liberty.** If the confidence/likelihood outputs are shown to be
   purely administrative (caseload triage) and **never** a basis for entry/arrest/detention, the harm
   pathway (item 3) weakens to risk-only. **Status: contested** — reporting + testimony indicate use to
   direct apprehension and as warrant-adjacent justification; disclosure of use-policy would resolve it.

3. **Error rates are low and audited.** If independently audited false-positive rates are negligible and
   the inputs are validated, the "error-laden data laundered into certainty" limb weakens. **Status:
   OPEN** — undisclosed (item N-2). Capturable only by the respondent/agency.

## Conditions that would SHRINK (not defeat) the charge

4. **Attribution holds fully for ICE.** To the degree a court assigns the liberty deprivation wholly to
   ICE's independent judgment, Palantir's share narrows — but the **instrument-maker's contestability
   contribution** persists (the score remains uninspectable regardless of who relied on it). Already
   conceded in `00`/`03`.

5. **The PARTIAL items fall.** If the "100 percent" quote and the Medicaid-data feed are disproven, the
   case loses **nothing load-bearing** — both are already filed PARTIAL and excluded from the spine.

## Conditions that would STRENGTHEN it (named for symmetry, not assumed)

- A court ruling that confidence scores can't meet the warrant standard they're used for.
- A disclosed false-positive rate that is non-trivial.
- A documented case where a wrong score drove a wrongful seizure with no correction path (the ACLU SoCal
  suit may supply this; pending its record).

## The discipline

Step one asks for: the contract's performance-work-statement, the data-source list, the
confidence-score methodology, the audit/error records, and proof of an effective contest mechanism.
**Every defeater above is resolved by producing exactly that record.** A charge that **dissolves on the
disclosure it demands** is built right: if the disclosure exonerates, the demand was still legitimate
and the answer is the record.

**The Tier-4 line is the permanent concession:** anyone citing this as proof of a foreclosed
discernment commons is refuted by the live congressional, advocacy, press, and litigation record. Held
to Tier 3 + contestability.
