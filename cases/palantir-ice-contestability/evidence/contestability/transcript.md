## Establishes (THE CONTESTABILITY GAP)
- An ICE agent ("J.B.") testified about **ELITE** (Palantir), described as like "Google Maps" showing
  locations of people who may be deportable and the **likelihood** they live there.
- Reporting: "A numerical likelihood score ... does not, on its own, establish that a person is present
  inside a particular residence ... ELITE's confidence scores are not well suited to satisfying the
  constitutional standards required for obtaining judicial warrants to enter homes."
- The liberty-depriving determination rests on a score the subject cannot inspect or contest, used
  beyond the standard it can bear. (The reported "could say 100 percent, and it's wrong" line is filed
  PARTIAL in 01/02 and is NOT relied on.)
