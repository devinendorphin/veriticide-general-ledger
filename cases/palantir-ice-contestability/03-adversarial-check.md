# 03 — ADVERSARIAL CHECK

*Mandatory. Palantir's defense steel-manned at full strength (the counter-register), then what
survives. Counter-register logged beside the charge, never suppressed.*

## The defense, steel-manned

**D-1 — "We are a neutral tool; the agency decides."** Palantir builds software; ICE officers make
apprehension and detention decisions and bear the legal duty (warrants, probable cause). A vendor is not
the state actor. *(This is the strongest defense — the attribution joint.)*

**D-2 — "Analytics ≠ the deprivation."** A confidence score is an input to human judgment, not the
seizure itself; agents, courts, and supervisors stand between the score and any liberty deprivation.

**D-3 — "Ethics safeguards exist."** Palantir maintains a Privacy & Civil Liberties function and
internal review; it argues its tooling can also *reduce* error versus ad-hoc methods.

**D-4 — "Lawful contract, lawful purpose."** A sole-source federal award to support immigration
enforcement — prioritizing "violent criminals, gang members, visa overstays" — is facially lawful
government procurement.

**D-5 — "The errors are ICE's data, not our model."** Garbage-in from federal databases is the
government's data-quality problem, not the platform's.

## What survives the steel-man

- **D-1 / D-2 narrow the respondent's share — they do not zero it.** The charge is pitched **exactly**
  at the surviving contribution: building/operating an instrument whose **outputs cannot be contested by
  the subject**. ICE's culpability as wielder is **not reduced** by charging the instrument-maker's
  distinct act (cf. `x-bot-swarm` attribution concession). "A human is in the loop" is not a cure when
  the human is handed an **uninspectable score** dressed as fact — the loop cannot correct what it
  cannot see.
- **D-3 is logged and bounded.** An ethics function is a CONTROL on the *claim of indifference*, not a
  refutation of the contestability gap. The testable question — published error rates, an effective
  **subject access/correction/appeal** path — is exactly what the case demands and what is **absent**.
  Safeguards asserted but unauditable do not close the gap.
- **D-4 is conceded** (`00`, BOUNDARY): lawfulness of the contract is not contested; the charge is the
  **contestability failure**, not the procurement. Lawful purpose ≠ contestable instrument.
- **D-5 cuts toward the charge, not away.** Conceding the inputs are "full of errors" while building a
  system that turns them into **near-real-time targeting scores used to justify home entry** is the
  case: the platform **launders error-laden data into operational certainty**. Whose data it is does
  not restore the subject's ability to contest the output.

## The over-reach this case must refuse (turned on itself)

- **Do not call this veriticide.** Recognition functions (Congress, ACLU, EFF, NPR, courts). Held to
  **Tier 3 + contestability register**.
- **Do not assert Palantir decides deportations** — attribution conceded; charge the instrument.
- **Do not assert intent to harm** — knowing operation only.
- **Do not lean on the "100 percent" quote** (PARTIAL) or the Medicaid-feed report (PARTIAL); lead with
  the warrant-standard point and the signed contracts.

## Net

A **bounded, survivable** step-one charge: a real, contracted, fused-data targeting instrument whose
liberty-depriving outputs are uncontestable, with the neutral-tool defense conceded at the attribution
joint and the charge pitched precisely at the surviving contribution. Weakest joints — attribution, the
PARTIAL quote/Medicaid items, the undisclosed error rate — are on the face of the matrix, not hidden.
