## What this matrix establishes, and does not

**Establishes:** that the actor with the greatest power over the evidentiary field, who was
**also the head of the affected agency**, issued an authoritative denial — escalating to "a lie"
— against a closed record generated inside his own agency, and maintained it through formal
challenge by non-response. That is the Art. II(2)(d) act performed with constructive knowledge
(II(3)(d)) against a legible record (II(3)(c)).

**Does not establish:** that Rubio caused any death (not charged); specific intent (not required);
or that a court will accept the State-Department-as-instrument characterization (the contested
beam, II(3)(a)).
