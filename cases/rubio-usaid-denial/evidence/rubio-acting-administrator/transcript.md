## Content (search-surfaced)

- **Feb 3, 2025:** President Trump appointed Secretary of State **Marco Rubio as Acting
  Administrator of USAID** — "an interim step toward gaining control … over the agency's
  activity." Rubio confirmed the State Department's de-facto takeover of the agency.
- Rubio **held the acting USAID administrator role until August 2025** — i.e., across the entire
  period in which the named deaths occurred, the Tamlyn cable was sent, the ProPublica internal
  memos were generated, and his "no one has died" testimony was given (May 21).

## Why this is the keystone of the Rubio case

The DOGE packet could establish that warnings traveled *through* Rubio's chain of command but
**not** that he personally read them. This fact narrows that gap structurally:

- As **Acting Administrator of USAID**, Rubio was the **head of the very agency** whose
  dismantlement produced the deaths he denied.
- The **Tamlyn cable** (Apr 21) was from **his own ambassador**; the **ProPublica internal
  memos** warning of "hundreds of thousands of deaths" were generated by **his own agency's
  senior leadership**. These were not adjacent channels — they were **his direct line
  responsibility**.
- Under Art. II(3)(d), where an actor arranges or occupies a position such that the warning is
  his own institution's, **engineered ignorance is constructive knowledge**. A Cabinet officer
  who is also the agency's Acting Administrator cannot locate himself outside his own agency's
  warnings.

## What it establishes

- **Authorization + role (Track B):** Rubio was simultaneously the denier and the institutional
  head of the dismantled agency.
- **Mental element (Art. II(3)(d)):** the strongest available basis for constructive knowledge —
  the warnings were internal to the agency he ran.

## Falsification hooks

Falls if Rubio's appointment as Acting Administrator post-dates the relevant warnings (it does
not — Feb 3 precedes the cable, the memos, and the testimony), or if the role was purely nominal
with a firewall insulating him from USAID's internal reporting (a firewall the actor would have
to establish, and which would itself be an engineered-ignorance arrangement under II(3)(d)).
