## Why this is the sharpest single act in the case

The Convention's Art. II(2)(d) names "the systematic rendering inadmissible, pathological, or
conspiratorial of testimony that names the conduct." There is an **escalation ladder** in the
denial, and this item is its apex:

1. *"No one has died because of USAID cuts."* — bare verdict (May 21).
2. *"No children are dying on my watch."* — bare verdict, possessive form.
3. Of the Boston University modeling: *"false, that's fake."* — branding the foreseeability
   evidence fake.
4. **Branding the truthful testimony itself "a lie."** — this is no longer denial; it is the
   **inversion** that recodes the witnesses (reporters, modelers, the bereaved) as the liars.
   That is the purest form of the Art. II(2)(d) act: rendering the testimony not merely
   inadmissible but *dishonest*.
