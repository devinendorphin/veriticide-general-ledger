# 04 — FALSIFICATION MEMO

*The conditions under which this packet is wrong, stated in advance. Given that it documents a private
individual from an operator-curated corpus, falsifiability and the reflexivity condition are not
ornamental — they are the packet's conscience.*

> Re-run this memo against every revision. If the §Reflexivity condition is not honored on a given
> use, the packet must not be brought.

---

## A. What would falsify the **specimens** (the acts)

1. **Misquotation.** Any specimen's verbatim is shown to be misquoted, cropped, or fabricated relative
   to the original post. (Custody is SCREENSHOT-HELD with **no canonical URL** — so this risk is real
   and is the first thing a canonical-URL capture should check.)
2. **Marked satire.** A specimen is shown to carry an in-post satirical marker (turn/reversal/visible
   target) that the extract missed — converting it from assertion-at-scale to protected satire (the
   grooming post is the one to re-test).
3. **Context defeats the move.** A specimen quotes/reacts to a specific sourced event (e.g., the
   "eating children" post turns out to cite a named court case) such that it is reporting, not
   conspiracy amplification.

## B. What would falsify the **register/pattern claims**

1. **One-off, not register.** The "chemical castration" same-date repetition is shown to be a single
   thread or quote rather than two independent posts — collapsing Finding 1's distribution claim.
2. **Registers are mixed (or not separated).** Posts are found that mix affirmation and pathologizing
   *within one post*, defeating the "structurally separated registers" tell — **or**, conversely, a
   post explicitly bridging the registers would *strengthen* (the ledger's CLOSED condition). Either
   way the current PARTIAL status is the honest state.

## C. What would (correctly) keep the account verdict declined

The verdict is **already declined** and this memo does not seek to close it. It would only ever move
toward an account-level inference on: a substantially larger, **independently captured** corpus (not
operator-curated); persistence of the pathologizing register **after** Jan 2026; or an explicit
register-bridge post. Absent these, **declined is the correct and stable state** — and even with them,
the protected-speech wall (`03`, Defense 1) governs.

## D. The CONTROL / counter-evidence condition

Counter-evidence is **already on record and preserved** (5 CE + NULL + SINCERE + the affirmative
meta-post context). What would further strengthen the *counter* side: a post from the account using
**clinical** rather than pathologizing vocabulary for medical transition, or **debunking** the
Satanic-panic/grooming framings, or defending LGBTQ+ educators against the grooming frame. Any such
post is logged as counter-evidence beside the specimens, never subtracted by erasure.

## E. The reflexivity condition (the packet turning in the hand) — load-bearing

**The analyst's stake is real and declared.** The operator who compiled this corpus posts as
**@glubose / @devinendorphin** and is a **participant**: the companion counter-speech is his, and at
least one specimen ("death threats… Trans, and Muslims") names the speaker's antagonists in a feud the
operator is part of. The corpus is **his selection of screenshots**. This creates two uncured risks:

1. **Selection effect** — a curated subset can manufacture a pattern that an independent capture would
   not show. (Mitigated by preserving hostile *and* affirmative content; **not cured**.)
2. **Interested framing** — branding an antagonist's protected speech as veriticide to win an
   interpersonal dispute is precisely an Art. V(3) self-referential failure. If this packet does that
   — if it suppresses a genuine rebuttal/satire, or asserts a verdict it has declined, or treats the
   operator's counter-speech as above scrutiny — it **falls within its own terms** and the finding
   must be struck.

**The condition:** this packet may be used only as *operator-curated specimens with the account
verdict declined and the stake declared.* The inference it would take to go further — an independent,
complete capture of the account — is **absent**, and the packet says so rather than papering over it.

## F. Custody falsifier

Because every item is **SCREENSHOT-HELD** with no canonical URL, the entire evidentiary base is one
step weaker than any other packet in the repo. A single demonstrated screenshot manipulation would
impeach the corpus's integrity. The mitigation is the hashed verbatim transcripts; the cure (canonical
URLs + original-form capture) is **deliberately not pursued** here for a private individual and is
recorded as a known, accepted limitation, not a to-do.
