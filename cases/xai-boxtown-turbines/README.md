## The knife, in brief

To power its Colossus AI supercomputer, **xAI** installed and ran **on the order of dozens of methane
gas turbines** in **South Memphis** for roughly a year **without ordinary Clean Air Act permits**,
emitting NOₓ and formaldehyde into **Boxtown** — a majority-Black, ozone-non-attainment community
already carrying one of the region's heaviest pollution burdens. The Shelby County Health Department
issued a permit for **15 "permanent backup" turbines** on **July 2, 2025**, while treating the prior
unpermitted units under a contested **"nonroad engine"** theory. SELC, for the **NAACP** and **Young,
Gifted & Green**, filed a stay-triggering **appeal on July 16, 2025**.
