# 00 — CHARGE THEORY

*One page. The AI-energy material-harm case: a genuine emitting instrument run at scale, without
permits, beside an already-overburdened protected population — the harm laundered through a
regulatory exemption and a "backup/negligible" frame, but **not** rendered undiscernable (recognition
is live). A Tier 1–3 knife, deliberately **not** inflated to Tier 4.*

---

## The charge, in one sentence

**To power its "Colossus" AI supercomputer in South Memphis, xAI installed and ran on the order of
dozens of methane gas turbines for roughly a year **without the Clean Air Act permits** such major
emitting sources ordinarily require — emitting NOₓ (a smog precursor) and formaldehyde into Boxtown,
a majority-Black community already in ozone non-attainment and already carrying one of the region's
heaviest pollution burdens — while the conduct was laundered as permissible through (a) a "nonroad /
temporary engine" exemption claim that treated installed, continuously-running turbines as outside the
Act, and (b) a "backup-only, state-of-the-art controls, negligible impact" frame advanced alongside a
"business continuity" and jobs/tax narrative.**

The chargeable inversion is the **conjunction**: a real, permit-triggering emitting instrument
operated **as if** it were exempt, in the one place its marginal burden lands hardest.

## The spine (order of proof)

> Prove the **instrument** is genuine and emitting (turbines, capacity, fuel) · prove it ran
> **without the ordinary permit** (the year of unpermitted operation; the exemption determination) ·
> prove the **legibility** of harm (Memphis non-attainment + Boxtown's pre-existing burden = the harm
> was knowable before deployment) · prove the **realized/risked harm** (NOₓ, formaldehyde into an
> EJ community) · mark the **laundering frame** (exemption + "backup/negligible" + "business
> continuity") · and **bound it honestly**: recognition was contested in real time, so the Tier-4
> overlay is weak — charge the material harm, not a discernment-kill.

## The protected interest

The **bodily and environmental viability** of a delineable population: residents of Boxtown / 38109
South Memphis — predominantly Black, historically the sited destination for the region's heavy
industry — whose air is already in ozone non-attainment and whose reported cancer/asthma burden is
elevated. The group is delineated **downstream off the demonstrated/risked exposure**, not off any
perpetrator's stated target.

## The instrument (Art. II(3)(a))

A genuine industrial emitting system — multiple methane combustion turbines generating on the order of
hundreds of MW to feed a single AI training cluster. This is not a metaphorical instrument; it is
combustion hardware. (Contrast the redistricting packet, where the instrument is software computing an
effect; here the instrument **is** the emission source.)

## Tier placement — **bounded, and this is the case's spine**

- **Tier 1–3 — material / subgraph harm: MET (pending air-monitoring confirmation).** A delineable,
  already-overburdened population bears a fresh, foreseeable pollution load.
- **Tier 4 — veriticide proper: WEAK / NOT MET.** The recognition commons **functioned**: the appeal,
  the NAACP's Clean Air Act action, 1,700–2,000+ public comments, and sustained national press named
  the harm contemporaneously. The administrative appeal was later **denied on "mootness"
  (~2025-12-15)** — an administrative closure, **not** a merits vindication — and recognition then
  **relocated to federal court (2026 NAACP emergency action)** rather than being foreclosed. The
  "nonroad"/"negligible" frames were **attempted** laundering that did **not** foreclose recognition.
  Charging Tier 4 here would be the over-reach this project exists to refuse. *(Whether the mootness
  denial itself functions as a proceduralism closure is a question for the court — flagged, not
  asserted.)*

## The respondent — and the enabling condition (kept distinct)

**The respondent (base offence): xAI** — the operator that installed and ran the instrument and
advanced the exemption/"backup" frame.

**The enabling condition (documented, NOT charged): the Shelby County Health Department's**
"nonroad/Clean-Air-Act-inapplicable" determination, and the **Greater Memphis Chamber / city**
"business continuity"/jobs framing. These are logged at the recognition layer for their on-record
**effect** (lowering the bar the operator had to clear). **Regulatory-capture intent is OPEN and not
asserted** — the SCHD may be charged in the court of public review via the live appeal, which is the
proper forum. This packet does not name SCHD as a perpetrator.

## What this charge theory does NOT establish

- **Not Tier-4 veriticide** — recognition was live; see above. This is the explicit anti-over-reach line.
- **Not intent to harm** — only knowing operation of foreseeably-burdensome unpermitted equipment.
- **Not that backup generation is per se wrongful** — hospitals/industry run similar units lawfully
  (the Chamber's true point); the charge is the **unpermitted, at-scale, EJ-sited** conjunction, not
  combustion as such. **CONTROL:** a permitted, genuinely cold-standby, BACT-controlled 15-turbine
  configuration in a non-overburdened airshed would **not** be this case.
- **Not that the controls/removals are fictitious** — 12 turbines were reportedly removed (May 2025)
  and the permanent 15 are claimed BACT-controlled backup; these are CONTROLS weighed in `03`, not
  buried.
- **Not a verdict** — step one: a basis to demand the emissions data, the permit record, independent
  air monitoring, and EPA Region 4 review. Not a tribunal finding.
