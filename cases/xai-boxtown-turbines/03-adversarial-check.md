# 03 — ADVERSARIAL CHECK

*Mandatory. xAI's / the boosters' defense steel-manned at full strength (the counter-register), then
what survives it. The counter-register is logged **beside** the charge, never suppressed.*

## The defense, steel-manned

**D-1 — "Backup only, with state-of-the-art controls; impact negligible."** The permitted 15 are
characterized as cold/limited backup with BACT-grade emissions controls. If true and enforced, the
permitted configuration's marginal airshed contribution may be small. *(Strong on the permanent
config; weaker on the year of unpermitted running.)*

**D-2 — "We're removing the temporary turbines."** Mayor Young stated 12 of the remaining 20 were
removed in May 2025, with the rest to follow. If completed, the acute unpermitted phase is
self-terminating. *(Real mitigation — but timeline "unclear," completion unverified.)*

**D-3 — "Everyone does this; it's business continuity."** The Chamber's point that hospitals,
manufacturers, and other facilities lawfully operate similar on-site generation is **true**, and is the
case's most important CONTROL: combustion backup is not per se wrongful.

**D-4 — "The Clean Air Act didn't apply (nonroad/temporary engines)."** SCHD itself adopted this
reading. It is a colorable regulatory position, not a fringe one — which is why the dispute is in an
appeal, not a slam-dunk enforcement action.

**D-5 — "Jobs and tax base."** Hundreds of jobs and ~$30M first-year tax revenue are a genuine public
benefit for a city that sought the investment.

## What survives the steel-man

- **D-1 / D-2 narrow the charge to the unpermitted phase — they don't erase it.** The chargeable
  conduct is **a year of dozens of turbines running without the permit** in a non-attainment airshed.
  Even if the permanent 15 are clean and the temporaries fully leave, the **already-occurred**
  exposure to an overburdened community is the act. Mitigation going forward is a sentencing factor,
  not an acquittal.
- **D-3 is conceded and built into the charge theory** (see `00`, BOUNDARY + CONTROL). The charge is
  **not** combustion; it is the **conjunction** — *unpermitted × at-scale × non-attainment ×
  EJ-sited*. Strip the conjunction and there is no case; the conjunction is present.
- **D-4 is exactly what's contested** and is filed as contested (`01` item 3, PARTIAL). The case does
  **not** assert a proven violation; it asserts a **colorable, live dispute** sufficient for step one
  (demand the record, the monitoring, EPA review). The fact that SCHD had to invoke an exemption
  **concedes the turbines would otherwise need a permit** — which is the predicate, not a defense.
- **D-5 is real and does not bear on harm.** Jobs/tax is a **benefit reframe** (Move 6); it is logged,
  and it answers "was there upside," not "was a protected population exposed without the ordinary
  permit." The two are not commensurable; the asymmetry tell is treating the second as cancelled by
  the first.

## The over-reach this case must refuse (turned on itself)

- **Do not call this veriticide.** Recognition functioned (appeal, suit, press, comments). Asserting
  Tier 4 here would be the unfalsifiable-overlay move. Held to **Tier 1–3**.
- **Do not assert intent to harm.** Only knowing operation of foreseeably-burdensome unpermitted
  equipment. Malice is neither needed nor claimed.
- **Do not pin a turbine number.** "Dozens" present is supported; an exact count is not. Use the range.
- **Do not launder the "4× cancer risk" figure as own-verified.** It is advocacy/agency-cited (PARTIAL).

## Net

A **bounded, survivable** step-one charge: a real emitting instrument run unpermitted at scale beside
an overburdened protected population, with the laundering frames (exemption + "negligible" + "business
continuity" + jobs) named and the genuine mitigations (removals, controls) credited. The case is
**strong at Tier 1–3 and explicitly declines Tier 4.** Its weakest joints — exact count, exemption
legality, risk-figure provenance — are conceded on the face of the matrix, not hidden.
