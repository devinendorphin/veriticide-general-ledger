status. Grades: **P1** = party/primary (permit record, legal filing, company/government statement,
EPA designation); **P2** = quality journalism; **ADV** = advocacy source (party-primary for its own
legal claims, advocacy for characterizations). Status: VERIFIED (multi-source) · PARTIAL (single/
contested/advocacy-cited) · OPEN.*
