# 04 — FALSIFICATION MEMO

*What would defeat, or materially shrink, this charge. Written before any defender writes it for us. If
a condition below is met, the corresponding element falls — and the matrix is built so each is
checkable.*

## Conditions that would DEFEAT the core charge

1. **The exemption is legally correct.** If the Air Pollution Control Board (or a court) rules the
   turbines genuinely fell under a valid "nonroad/temporary engine" exemption, the
   **unpermitted-operation predicate (item 3)** collapses into "lawful interim operation," and the case
   reduces to a siting/EJ grievance without the regulatory-evasion spine. **Status: OPEN, but weakened** —
   EPA has confirmed such turbines require permits (`epa-action`), cutting against the exemption read,
   and the Dec-2025 board denial was on **mootness, not the merits**, so it did not validate the
   exemption. Still the most likely defeater, now less likely.

2. **Independent air monitoring shows no marginal degradation.** If ambient NOₓ/formaldehyde
   monitoring around Boxtown during the unpermitted phase shows no attributable increase over baseline,
   the **realized-harm limb** weakens to risk-only. (Legibility/risk would survive; realized harm would
   not.) **Status: OPEN — the data demand is the remedy this case asks for.**

3. **The "dozens" were never actually operated.** If the on-site turbines seen in imagery were
   substantially staged/non-operational (vs. running), the instrument's **scale** shrinks toward the
   Chamber's "15 operational." Presence is verified; **operation at the upper count is PARTIAL.**

## Conditions that would SHRINK (not defeat) the charge

4. **Full, prompt removal of all temporary turbines + clean permanent config.** Credited as mitigation
   (`03` D-1/D-2); converts the case from ongoing to historical. The already-occurred exposure remains
   the act.

5. **The "4× cancer risk" figure is overstated.** If independent derivation puts Boxtown's excess risk
   materially lower, the **EJ-burden framing (item 5)** weakens but does not vanish — non-attainment +
   documented industrial siting independently support an overburdened-population finding. The figure is
   already filed **PARTIAL** precisely so the case does not lean on it.

## Conditions that would STRENGTHEN it (named, for symmetry — not assumed)

- A board/court ruling that the turbines required a permit (kills D-4).
- Monitoring data showing attributable exposure (promotes item 5/realized harm to VERIFIED).
- Evidence the upper turbine count was operational (resolves item 1 toward the high range).

## The discipline

This case **does not need** the maximal version of any contested fact to assert **step one**. Step one
asks for: the permit record, the emissions/monitoring data, and EPA Region 4 review. Every defeater
above is resolved by **producing exactly that record** — which is why the remedy is disclosure and
audit, not a verdict. A charge that is **dissolved by the data it demands** is a charge built right: if
the data exonerates, the demand was still legitimate and the answer is the data.
