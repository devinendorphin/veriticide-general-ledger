## Establishes (FORESEEABILITY / Track C)
That the Shelby County Health Department was put on written notice, as early as 2024-08-26, that xAI/CTC
was operating gas turbines without permits. This dates the legibility/knowledge element to ~14 months
before the permit issued (2025-07-02) and ~10 months before the appeal — the regulator's awareness was
contemporaneous with the unpermitted operation, not retrospective.
