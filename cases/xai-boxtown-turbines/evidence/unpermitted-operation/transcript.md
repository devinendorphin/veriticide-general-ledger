## Verbatim / near-verbatim excerpts preserved
- xAI installed and ran methane gas turbines to power its Colossus data center; turbines operated
  "without appropriate permits" for roughly a year.
- The Shelby County Health Department (SCHD) issued the air permit on July 2, 2025, allowing xAI to run
  15 methane gas turbines 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
- SCHD "wrongly claimed that the federal Clean Air Act didn't apply to xAI's unpermitted gas turbines,
  citing an exemption for 'nonroad engines.'"
- More than 2,000 comments were sent to SCHD by residents, most opposing the turbines.
- South Memphis neighborhoods "face elevated asthma rates and cancer risks four times the national
  average"; the Memphis area is in non-attainment for smog (ozone).
