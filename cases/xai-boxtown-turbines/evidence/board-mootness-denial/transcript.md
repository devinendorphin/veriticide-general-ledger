# board-mootness-denial — Air Pollution Control Board denies appeal (mootness)

Canonical: https://www.actionnews5.com/2025/12/16/shelby-co-air-pollution-control-board-denies-appeal-over-xais-turbine-use-citing-mootness/
Bears date: 2025-12-16 (decision ~2025-12-15) · Grade: S1

## Establishes (DISPOSITION)
- The Memphis & Shelby County Air Pollution Control Board DENIED the appeal over xAI's gas-turbine
  permits, citing "mootness," after a ~7-hour hearing (decision ~Dec 15, 2025).
- xAI presented data showing "no high levels of pollution near or around the communities."
- Young, Gifted & Green and community members opposed.

## Disposition note (honest framing)
The administrative path closed on mootness; it did NOT vindicate the turbines on the merits, and
litigation continued in federal court (see epa-action). "Mootness" is logged as a disposition, not as a
merits finding either way; whether it functions as a proceduralism closure is an open question for the
court, not asserted here.
