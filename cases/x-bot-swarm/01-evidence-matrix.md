## What this matrix establishes, and does not

**Establishes:** that the platform owner built an incentive/amplification architecture that
foreseeably rewards manufactured consensus, that coordinated inauthentic amplification persisted on
the platform (including on his own posts) after the purge was framed as won, and that the
independent measurement which could test the purge verdict was priced/shut out — the Art. II(2)(d)
act (habitat + verdict + foreclosure) performed with constructive knowledge (II(3)(d)) against a
legible record (II(3)(c)) by a system-scale instrument (II(3)(a)).

**Does not establish:** that Musk runs/funds/directs any specific swarm (not charged); the
identity or paymaster of any operator/lift-client; or a platform-wide prevalence figure (foreclosed
by the measurement shutdown itself). The attribution of any individual swarm is the contested beam
(`03-adversarial-check.md`).
