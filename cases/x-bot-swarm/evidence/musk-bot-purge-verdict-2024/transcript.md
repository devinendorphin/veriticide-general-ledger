## A. The verdict (the crackdown declared / framed as accomplished)

- Musk, on first moving to acquire the platform, "emphasized the dire effects of bot accounts, and
  **pledged to remove them as one of his first acts as owner**" (restofworld, summarizing the arc
  from the 2022 promise).
- **April 2024:** Musk announced **"a system purge of bots & trolls,"** which the platform's
  official **@Safety** account described as **"a significant, proactive initiative to eliminate
  accounts that violate our Rules against platform manipulation and spam."**
- **April 9, 2026:** Head of Product **Nikita Bier** stated X was **"identifying and suspending 208
  bots per minute"** — the crackdown re-asserted as an ongoing, quantified success metric.

These are the explicit "the bots are being handled / defeated" statements charged as the bare-verdict
half of the Art. II(2)(d) act — the consensus-layer sibling of the Rubio "no one has died" verdict.

## B. The verdict against the record (why it is the act, not a report)

- **restofworld (2024):** "the fruits of those efforts have been **slow to ripen**, and **both spam
  and sock-puppet accounts remain common** on the platform." Documented persistence *during the
  purge*: Clemson University found **460+ coordinated accounts** around Rwanda's July 15, 2024
  election ("when reported to X, **nothing happened**"); Global Witness tracked **610,000+ bot-like
  posts** during the U.K.'s July 4, 2024 election (X "only sent automated replies").
- **Social Media Today (2026):** "**external reports suggest the opposite**" of Musk's declarations
  that X had resolved bot issues; a 2023 **University of Queensland** report found X "is **flooded
  with platform manipulation**," and the **European Commission** identified X as having "the
  **largest ratio of mis/disinformation posts**" among major platforms; the problem has "**actually
  worsened**."

The verdict is therefore asserted against a contemporaneous record of persistence — and (per the
companion item `research-api-foreclosure-2023`) while the independent measurement that could test
it had been priced/shut out. A self-declared, quantified success ("208/min") that cannot be
externally audited is the bare-verdict + foreclosure structure the case charges.
