## Instance 1 — Doppelganger / Social Design Agency (court + sanctions; X named) — Sept 4, 2024

- **Operator:** Russian companies **Social Design Agency (SDA)**, **Structura National Technology**,
  and **ANO Dialog**, operating "under the direction and control of the Russian Presidential
  Administration," specifically First Deputy Chief of Staff **Sergei Vladilenovich Kiriyenko**.
  Founders sanctioned by U.S. Treasury: **Ilya Andreevich Gambashidze** (SDA), **Nikolai
  Aleksandrovich Tupikin** (Structura).
- **Client:** the **Russian Presidential Administration** (state principal).
- **Message-set / method:** ~60 spoofed news-site domains plus "fictitious social media accounts,"
  with operatives posing as Americans/others "posting comments on social media platforms, **mainly X
  and Meta**," linking to the spoofed domains; goal "**reducing international support for Ukraine**,"
  bolstering pro-Russian interests, and influencing U.S. and foreign elections.
- **Price:** state-directed/financed; the operation is tied to a commercial advertising firm and
  documented project contracts (ISD). U.S. action: DOJ seized **32 internet domains** (Sept 4,
  2024); Treasury sanctions on the two founders.

## Instance 2 — Team Jorge / AIMS (the priced exemplar; Twitter named) — exposed Feb 15, 2023

- **Operator:** **Tal Hanan** ("Jorge"), former Israeli special-forces operative, running a team of
  "graduates of government agencies" from six offices.
- **Software:** **AIMS (Advanced Impact Media Solutions)** — controls "thousands of fake social
  media profiles on **Twitter**, LinkedIn, Facebook, Telegram, Gmail, Instagram and YouTube"; some
  avatars carry credit cards, crypto wallets, and Airbnb accounts.
- **Client:** offered to "intelligence agencies, political campaigns and private companies that
  wanted to secretly manipulate public opinion"; the team claimed to have meddled in **33
  presidential elections** (27 "successful").
- **Message-set:** client-defined covert manipulation of public opinion (election outcomes).
- **Price:** **€6m–€15m per election interference** (Hanan, to undercover reporters). Investigation:
  Forbidden Stories consortium; ~6 hours of meetings secretly recorded **July–December 2022**;
  published Feb 15, 2023.

Sources:

- Gizmodo, "A Secretive Israeli Company Says It Manipulated More Than 30 Elections Worldwide" — https://gizmodo.com/team-jorge-election-meddling-tal-hanan-misinformation-1850120328

## Instance 3 — Meliorator / RT bot farm (X-only; court action) — July 9–10, 2024

- **Operator:** **RT** (Russian state media) affiliates, using AI software **"Meliorator"** that
  generates fake profiles and the disinformation they post; **Russia's FSB had access** to the
  software.
- **Client / financier:** "**approved and financed by the Kremlin**."
- **Message-set:** disinformation "to and about the U.S., Poland, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain,
  Ukraine and Israel."
- **Platform:** "**As of June 2024, Meliorator only worked on X**." DOJ/FBI (with Canada and the
  Netherlands) seized **two domains and 968 X accounts**.
- **Price:** state-financed (Kremlin). **Terminal-node tell:** X "**voluntarily suspended the
  remaining bot accounts** identified in the court documents" — i.e., action followed the court
  filing, echoing the case's "nothing happened when reported" pattern (Cyabra/GZERO; Rest of World).

Sources:

## Why these three, together

They supply the four attributes the case's contested node required — **operator, client,
message-set, price** — as named, dated, and (for two) court-established facts, on X specifically.
They confirm the operator's framing of the ecosystem ("there's other entities who control such
things and they're paid to lift up messages") is not speculative. They leave the Musk-attribution
question exactly where the case left it: **conceded and uncharged.** Their load-bearing function is
to convert the paid-lift node from "hypothetical" to "documented," and to add a second court-grade
instance of the terminal-node pattern (platform action only after external/legal force).
