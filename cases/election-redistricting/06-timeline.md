# 06 — TIMELINE

*The performed-ignorance arc, in order: the apparatus that could recognize vote dilution is removed
piece by piece across thirteen years, while the apparatus that computes the dilution gets more precise.*

**Types:** **MEAS** removal/limit of a measurement-or-remedy mechanism · **COMP** evidence the effect
is computed/admitted · **CTRL** a CONTROL (recognition occurs) · **HARM** realized harm / live dispute.

| Date | Type | Event | Item |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2013-06-25 | **MEAS** | *Shelby County v. Holder*, 570 U.S. 529 — §4(b) preclearance coverage formula struck ("things have changed"); the prospective mechanism that **measured** discriminatory voting changes goes dark | `shelby` |
| 2015 | **COMP** | GOP mapmaker's private study concludes a census citizenship question would let districts be drawn **"advantageous to Republicans and Non-Hispanic Whites"** — the effect computed, the rationale concealed | `hofeller-files` |

| 2019–2020 | **COMP** | Hofeller files published (Stephanie Hofeller via Common Cause/NPR); 4th Circuit had cited the study on the **pretext** question for the census citizenship add | `hofeller-files` |

| 2026-04-29 | **MEAS** | *Louisiana v. Callais*, No. 24-109 — §2 racial-vote-dilution enforcement **eviscerated**; stricter challenger standard imposed; the §2 path *Milligan* ran on is narrowed | `callais` |

## What the ordering establishes

1. **The measurement apparatus was removed in stages** while the **computing apparatus improved** —
   the defining shape of performed ignorance: not a sudden lie, but the patient production of a
   condition in which a quantified harm cannot be officially recognized.
2. **The receipts predate the unknowability.** The effect was computed (2015) and the target admitted
   (2016) **before** the holding that the effect is beyond judicial reach (2019) — the conjunction that
   makes the ignorance *performed*, not held.
3. **The CONTROL dates the closing.** *Milligan* (2023) shows the forum **could** still recognize
   dilution; *Callais* (2026) narrows that forum — so the CONTROL marks the last clear opening, and its
   narrowing is part of the arc, not a refutation of it.

## What this timeline does NOT establish

That any holding was decided with veriticidal intent (doctrinal intent open); that partisanship as
such is wrongful (the 2025 row is a discriminator, not a verdict); or the specific internal data behind
any single enacted map (the map-drawing working files are the upgrade, named in `05`). Dates are from
the public record; pull the slip opinions and dockets to fix any exact day at capture.
