## The charge, in one line

In U.S. redistricting, mapmakers and their vendors use precinct-level partisan and racial data and
optimization software to **compute** a plan's vote-diluting effect to high precision — and that
computed effect is then rendered **officially undiscernable as harm** by a doctrinal architecture that
declares it non-justiciable or presumptively innocent (**Shelby 2013 → Rucho 2019 → Alexander 2024 →
Callais 2026**) and by operators who **disavow** in public what they **calculated** in private
(the Hofeller mechanism) — laundering a foreseeable, quantified, at-scale reduction of a population's
political viability as either "just politics" or as unknowable.

## What this packet is, and is not

Per Standing Protocol §7 it asserts **step one** (a documented record + a basis to demand
preservation of map-drawing files and metrics, disclosure of the partisan/racial data used,
independent audit, and symmetric measurement) and **not** step two (a tribunal finding). It charges
the *structure*, concedes its hardest joints (race/party entanglement; doctrinal intent), and holds
