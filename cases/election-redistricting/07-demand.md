# 07 — STEP-ONE DEMAND (preservation · disclosure · audit · restraint)

*The instrument the packet exists to support. Per Standing Protocol §7, a record built under this
framework is "a basis to demand **preservation of evidence, disclosure of how a system is used,
independent audit, and provisional restraint**." This is that demand, as a model an affected
population or an organization accountable to one can adapt and serve. It asserts **step one** — not a
verdict (`00`, `03`).*

> **Symmetry is the discipline, not a caveat.** This demand is owed by **any** mapmaker who computed a
> map's partisan/racial effect, **regardless of party** — North Carolina (R) and Maryland (D) alike
> (both shielded by *Rucho*; see `evidence/rucho/`). A jurisdiction that **already publishes** the
> partisan/racial metrics of its enacted plan and drew to disclosed neutral criteria has, by that fact,
> **already satisfied** the disclosure demand — compliance dissolves the charge (`04` §E). The target is
> **performed ignorance over a computed effect**, never partisanship as such.

---

## To (adapt per jurisdiction)

- **[Redistricting authority]** — the legislature, redistricting committee, or commission that adopted **[the enacted plan / Plan No. ___]**, and its presiding officers and staff;
- **[Map vendor / consultant]** — any contracted mapmaker, firm, or expert who drew, scored, or advised on the plan (and any successor-custodian of a deceased consultant's files — *cf.* the Hofeller files, `evidence/hofeller-files/`);
- **[Data/IT custodians]** — officials holding the relevant datasets, software installations, project files, version history, and communications.

## Basis (why you are receiving this)

A documented record (Veriticide Master Ledger, Pattern Registry Entry 17 — *Performed Ignorance*;
`cases/election-redistricting/`) shows that redistricting plans are drawn with **precinct-level
partisan and racial data** and **map-scoring software** that quantify a plan's seat-level effect to
high precision, and that this computed effect has in documented instances been **disavowed** in public
while the apparatus that would measure it was removed (`evidence/`: Shelby 2013 → Rucho 2019 →
Alexander 2024 → Callais 2026; Hofeller 2015; Lewis 2016). This demand seeks to make the **computed
effect of [the enacted plan]** preservable and discernible. It does **not** assert that any individual
acted unlawfully; it asserts your **duty to preserve and disclose** what your own instruments produced.

---

## DEMAND 1 — PRESERVE (immediate litigation-hold)

Effective on receipt, suspend any routine deletion, overwrite, or destruction and **preserve in native
form** (with metadata and version history intact) all of the following relating to **[the enacted plan]**
and every draft considered:

1. **Map-drawing files** — every draft and enacted plan as **block-equivalency / block-assignment
   files**, shapefiles, and the native project files of any districting software used (e.g. Maptitude
   for Redistricting / Caliper, autoBound, Esri, DistrictBuilder, or equivalent), **including
   auto-save, undo-history, and prior versions**.
2. **The data layers loaded** — the precinct/block-level **partisan** indices (prior-election results,
   composite partisan scores, incumbency) and **racial/ethnic** data (CVAP, race by block) attached to
   the plan, and any modeled/imputed data (e.g. citizenship estimates) used.
3. **The scores computed** — every **partisan and racial metric** generated for any draft or enacted
   plan (e.g. seats/efficiency-gap/mean-median/partisan-bias figures; majority-minority counts;
   district-by-district racial and partisan composition), and any **ensemble or optimization output**
   (sets of candidate maps and their scores).
4. **Software and logs** — installation/version details, configuration, and any application or system
   logs evidencing what was computed and when.
5. **Communications and instructions** — emails, memos, texts, and meeting materials among mapmakers,
   officials, vendors, and consultants concerning the plan's partisan or racial effect or targets.
6. **Successor custody** — where a relevant consultant has died or departed, preserve and do not
   transfer or wipe their work files (the Hofeller precedent is the reason this clause exists).

Destruction or alteration of these materials after receipt may constitute **spoliation**.

## DEMAND 2 — DISCLOSE (how the system was used)

Within **[30 days / statutory public-records period]**, disclose:

1. the **data inputs** (Demand 1.2) actually used to draw and evaluate the plan;
2. the **partisan and racial metrics** (Demand 1.3) of the **enacted** plan and of the principal
   **alternatives considered**, including any alternative that achieved the same stated lawful
   objectives with a **smaller** racial/partisan effect;
3. **who** drew and scored the plan, under **what instructions**, and against **what criteria**;
4. whether partisan or racial data was **on screen** during drawing, and if disclaimed, the basis for
   the disclaimer in light of items 1–3.

*(In jurisdictions with public-records / FOIA-equivalent statutes, treat Demand 2 as a records request;
in active litigation, as a preservation-and-production demand.)*

## DEMAND 3 — INDEPENDENT AUDIT

Submit the enacted plan and the preserved materials to an **independent analysis** — or do not obstruct
one — comparing the plan's partisan and racial effect against **neutral computer-generated ensembles**
drawn to the jurisdiction's stated criteria, and **publish** the metrics. Where the enacted plan is a
statistical outlier on partisan or racial effect relative to the ensemble, say so.

## DEMAND 4 — PROVISIONAL RESTRAINT (where applicable)

Where a plan's computed effect was **disavowed** and remains unaudited, **do not lock it in for the next
election** before Demands 2–3 are met; at minimum, **publish the metrics before** that election so the
effect is discernible while intervention is still possible. (This tracks the realized, cascading harm in
`evidence/callais/` and `evidence/tx-ca-2025/`: districts removed for 2026 before the effect was made
publicly legible.)

---

## What compliance looks like (the CONTROL — and your exit)

You satisfy this demand — and **negate** the performed-ignorance finding as to your plan — by
**publishing** the partisan/racial metrics of the enacted plan and the alternatives, disclosing the data
and criteria used, and permitting the audit. An independent commission or court-appointed special master
who already does this (*cf. Allen v. Milligan*, `evidence/milligan-control/`) has nothing further to
disclose. **Disclosure is the remedy.** The only thing this demand cannot abide is the continued
production of unknowability around an effect your own instruments computed.

## What this demand does NOT do

It does not allege a crime, does not assert any individual's intent, does not ask you to stop drawing
partisan maps where the effect is **avowed and published**, and does not seek a verdict. It asks you to
**preserve and reveal what was computed** — the minimum precondition for the public to discern an effect
that is currently being performed as unknown.
