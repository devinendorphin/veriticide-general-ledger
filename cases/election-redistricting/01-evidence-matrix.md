## What this matrix establishes, and does not

**Establishes:** that the vote-diluting effect of a map is **computed** by an instrument in the
mapmaker's own hands (A); that the intent was **admitted** at the operator level and then **disavowed**
(B); that a doctrinal architecture then produced **official unknowability** of that effect and
**dismantled the apparatus** that measured it (C, D); and that the harm is **realized** with
partisanship serving as the actionable proxy for race (E) — the Performed-Ignorance structure, with
**legibility met at its strongest** (the effect is computed, not merely foreseeable).

**Does not establish:** that any **judge** acted with veriticidal intent (doctrinal intent open —
charged structurally); that **partisanship as such** is wrongful (the California row is a discriminator,
not a villain); or a verdict. Custody is **LOCATOR-VERIFIED** (canonical URLs identified; the P1
opinions and the Hofeller files are public and citable; original-form capture via `CAPTURE-RUNBOOK.md`).
