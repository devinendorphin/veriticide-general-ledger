## The charge, in one sentence

**In U.S. congressional and legislative redistricting, mapmakers and their vendors use precinct-level
partisan and racial data and optimization software to compute a districting plan's vote-diluting
effect to high precision — and that computed effect is then rendered officially undiscernable as harm
by (a) a doctrinal architecture that declares the effect non-justiciable or presumptively innocent
(Shelby 2013 → Rucho 2019 → Alexander 2024 → Callais 2026) and (b) operators who disavow in public
what they calculated in private (the Hofeller mechanism) — laundering a foreseeable, quantified,
at-scale reduction of a population's political viability as either "just politics" or as unknowable.**

This is **Performed Ignorance**: an effect an instrument in the actor's own hands already quantifies
(and operators admit intending) is reproduced as officially *unknown* at the exact point it would
create accountability — the operational form of Art. II(3)(d) ("engineered ignorance is constructive
knowledge").

## The spine (order of proof)

> Prove the effect is **computed** (the instrument quantifies it) · prove the intent is **admitted**
> at the operator level (Hofeller study; Lewis "10–3") · prove the **official unknowability** is
> produced (the doctrinal arc) · prove the **realized harm** (diluted representation; party-for-race
> proxy) · mark each actor's intent at the right altitude — operator admitted, doctrinal open —
> and charge the **structure**.

## The protected interest (Art. II(1))

Two limbs, indivisible: (1) the **political viability** of a delineable population — voters whose
ability to elect representatives of choice is reduced; in the racial-dilution line, Black and Latino
voters; the group is delineated **downstream, off the maps' demonstrated dilution** (Tier-3
evidentiary-delineation clause), not off any perpetrator's stated target; and (2) the **discernment
commons** — the shared capacity to recognize, contemporaneously, that vote dilution is occurring,
which the non-justiciability / good-faith / disentanglement / §2-evisceration doctrines attack.

## The instrument (Art. II(3)(a)) — a genuine system at scale

The **redistricting optimization apparatus**: precinct-level partisan and racial datasets plus
map-scoring software (the workflows documented in the Hofeller files and in partisan map programs)
that generate and grade enormous numbers of candidate maps against partisan/racial metrics — a system
producing locally defensible justifications ("compact," "communities of interest," "just politics")
at a scale and speed exceeding unaided human agency. A second, higher-order instrument operates at the
discernment layer: the **doctrinal apparatus** itself, whose precedents manufacture locally defensible
"non-justiciable" / "good-faith" justifications across every downstream case (cited under II(2)(d),
not as the base II(3)(a) instrument).

## The respondent — and the enabling condition (kept distinct)

**The respondent (base offence).** The named respondents are the **partisan mapmakers / vendors and the
legislatures that adopt their maps** — the operator level, where the instrument is run (II(3)(a)) and
where intent is **on the record** (II(3)(d): the Hofeller study, the Lewis admission). This is the
charged party.

**The enabling condition (NOT a charged respondent).** The **doctrinal apparatus** (Shelby → Rucho →
Alexander → Callais) is **not** named as a defendant, and the case seeks **no finding against any court
or judge**. It is documented **only** at the Art. II(2)(d) discernment-defeat layer — by its public,
on-the-record *effect* (the removal of the means to recognize the diluting effect) — with jurists'
intent **open and not asserted**. It is the environment in which the operator's performed ignorance
becomes unfalsifiable, not a co-perpetrator.

**Why the altitudes are kept apart.** Collapsing them is this packet's main over-reach risk
(`03-adversarial-check.md`, Defense 2 and the residual-soft-spots log): charge the **operator** for an
effect computed and admitted; document the **doctrine** for an effect it forecloses. The distributed,
multi-actor shape is the case's structural feature, conceded openly — operator-level ignorance is
*performed* (authored, dated: Hofeller/Lewis), while the systemic unknowability is *manufactured*
(institutional output, intent open). Neither claim is inflated into the other.

## What this charge theory does NOT establish

- That **partisanship as such** is wrongful — it is not; openly-avowed partisan maps where the effect
  is *published, not disavowed* are **not** this case (California Prop 50 CONTROL; `03`).
- That any **judge** acted with veriticidal intent — doctrinal intent is **open**; only operator-level
  intent (Hofeller/Lewis) is charged.
- That the system is **monolithic** — *Allen v. Milligan* (2023, §2 upheld) is the CONTROL where the
  dilution **was** recognized.
- A **verdict** — this is step one (a basis to demand preservation of map files/metrics, disclosure of
  the data used, and independent audit), not a tribunal finding.
