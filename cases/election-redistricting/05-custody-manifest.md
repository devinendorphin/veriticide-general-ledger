## Honest-custody notes

1. **Higher evidentiary floor, weaker custody form.** Unlike the social-media cases, the spine here is
   **P1 public-record opinions** plus a **published leaked corpus** — the facts are not in dispute. The
   only deficiency is that original-form bytes have not yet been hashed in this environment.
2. **The strongest item is not yet in hand.** The decisive upgrade is the **map-drawing working files
   and partisan/racial datasets** (the optimizer inputs that prove the effect was computed). Those are
   ordinarily withheld; their preservation/disclosure is the **step-one demand** this packet asserts,
   not something it claims already to hold.
3. **Doctrinal intent is uncaptured because uncharged.** No artifact here purports to show a judge's
   intent; the opinions are held for **holdings and effects**.
4. **Repo holds only integrity records.** Per `evidence/.gitignore`, any future original-form binaries
   (WARC/PDF/PNG) live in off-platform custody; only `manifest.json` / `sha256sums.txt` /
   `archive-org-snapshots.txt` are tracked.

## What this packet does not claim

It does not yet hold original-form captures (LOCATOR-VERIFIED, not VERIFIED); it does not hold the
underlying map-drawing files; it does not assert any judge's intent; and it does not charge partisanship
as such. It preserves citations and verbatim text for a structure built from public-record opinions and
a published leaked corpus, declares its limits, and stops there.
