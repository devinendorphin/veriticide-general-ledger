## Canonical primary source

- **Document:** Internal U.S. State Department diplomatic cable, **April 21, 2025**, from
  **U.S. Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Congo Lucy Tamlyn**.
- **Reported by:** Washington Post investigation — "Trump's USAID pause stranded lifesaving
  drugs. Children died waiting."
  - `https://www.washingtonpost.com/investigations/interactive/2025/usaid-trump-malaria-hiv-drugs-death/`

## Verbatim / near-verbatim content (search-surfaced)

- The "**abrupt elimination**" of PEPFAR-supported programs would lead to a **resurgence of
  HIV** in Congo and could lead to **new, drug-resistant strains** posing "a direct threat to
  the homeland." *(quoted phrase: "abrupt elimination")*
- The cable warned canceled programs could **jeopardize the lives of 28,000 adults and 2,500
  children** in PEPFAR-supported provinces.
- The effort had brought the country "**to the cusp of achieving HIV epidemic control**."
- Health commodities worth **$50 million** were "**still stranded**" in warehouses; only two of
  six large shipments of pediatric antiretroviral medication had reached regional warehouses by
  the end of June.

> *Realized-mortality data surfaced this pass (stronger than the ledger had):* in **Lualaba
> province, at least 219 HIV-positive people died between January and June 2025**, per
> preliminary data from Congolese health officials, **compared to 164** HIV-positive deaths in
> the same period the prior year. This is a before/after differential, not a projection.

> *Provenance note:* the bracketed phrases in quotation marks are reproduced verbatim as
> returned; the figures (28,000/2,500; $50M; 219 vs 164) are reproduced from the search
> synthesis of the WaPo investigation and must be confirmed against the article and, where
> possible, the cable itself during original-form capture.

## What it establishes

- **Foreseeability inside the chain (Art. II(3)(c)):** a warning of the specific harm (HIV
  resurgence, drug resistance, named casualty estimates) generated **inside the State
  Department's own diplomatic channel**, dated 30 days before Secretary Rubio's May 21
  congressional denial.
- **Statistical erasure (Art. IV(4)):** the Lualaba differential is cognizable distributional
  harm — a measured rise in deaths coincident with the withdrawal.
