## Content (search-surfaced)

- The letter expresses concern over "**the damage and loss of life**" Trump's executive order
  freezing foreign assistance would cause, and urges resumption of critical programs.
- Specifically flags: **PEPFAR** provides **20.6 million people across 55 countries** with
  antiretroviral treatment; the President's Malaria Initiative provides nets/medicines to 63
  million people; "**these lives depend on an uninterrupted supply of medicines**," and the
  pause is "expected to cost lives."
