## Why this artifact exists

The single strongest defense to the charge is: *"Lifesaving waivers were issued — PEPFAR was
exempted, so nothing was withdrawn."* This artifact preserves the record that the waivers were
**announced but materially inoperative**, and — decisively — that **DOGE personnel personally
operated the mechanism that prevented the exempted funds from flowing.** That converts the
rebuttal from "the waivers didn't work" into "the named respondent's own people made them not
work."

## The decisive fact (search-surfaced; confirm at original-form capture)

> USAID managers repeatedly prepared PEPFAR payments "**and got the agency's interim leaders to
> sign off on them with support from the White House**," but each time "**two DOGE employees who
> had been embedded at the State Department would veto the payments — a process that required
> them to manually check boxes in the payment system one at a time**."

This is a **Track A/B** fact: the named respondent (DOGE) is the proximate operator of the
withdrawal, *notwithstanding* the waiver. A waiver overridden box-by-box by DOGE is the
**reframing-as-care act itself** (Art. II(2)(c)): the exemption is announced while the
sustaining transfer is manually blocked.

## Supporting detail (search-surfaced)

- "A waiver is kind of useless without the ability to have some cash flow" — aid groups granted
  waivers could not draw funds from USAID's payment system.
- Former HIV/Ebola contractors called the waiver "**performative**" "**lip service**" as
  programs ground to a halt.
- **Haiti:** "128 sites, previously funded by PEPFAR, had completely halted services, out of
  181 total sites nationally."
- Communication bans with implementing partners; senior staff on leave; ⅔ of PMI contract staff
  fired; staff locked out of offices — the institutional capacity to *implement* a waiver was
  simultaneously dismantled.

## What it establishes

- **Defeats Defense 6** on the merits: the de jure exemption coexisted with a de facto
  withdrawal that DOGE actively maintained. The Art. II(2)(a) "withdrawal of conditions" is
  proven as **functional** withdrawal — exactly the laundered/administrative form Art. IV(4)
  (statistical erasure) exists to reach.
- **Strengthens authorization (Track B):** DOGE is not merely the framer; its embedded
  personnel are the proximate operators of the payment block.
