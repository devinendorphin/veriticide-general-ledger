## Precision notes (anchoring pass, 2026-06-24)

- **All rows now canonical-URL anchored** (the prior `ledger-ID` placeholders are resolved).
- **"$26B" clarified:** CREW attributes the $26B specifically to programs that **returned**
  funds to taxpayers — chiefly the **CFPB**, which "returned over $26 billion to the American
  people." State it as *taxpayer-benefit programs returning $26B were shuttered*, not "$26B of
  generic spending."
- **"$8.2B" provenance corrected:** the figure originates with the **USAID Office of Inspector
  General / watchdog** warning (Feb 2025), reported by Fortune, PBS, and WaPo — not primarily
  "USAFacts." The ledger's `doge-usafacts-usaid-newsletter` was a capture vehicle; the
  underlying source is the OIG report.
- **NYT scale upgrade:** the deletion behavior is larger than the ledger's "five biggest" —
  ~**1,000 entries (≈40%)** were erased or altered (Fahrenthold/NYT). This sharpens the
  deletion-not-correction / unauditable-by-design finding (Art. II(3)(a)).
