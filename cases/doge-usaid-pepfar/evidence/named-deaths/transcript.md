## Why a consolidated artifact

The named deaths are the case's answer to "these are only projections" (`../../03-adversarial-check.md`,
Defense 2). Each is an individual, reported by name, with cause linked to the funding
suspension. Together they establish realized harm independent of any model. Each row needs
original-form capture of its cited outlet.

## Demographic note (correction to "mostly children")

The subset is **disproportionately but not exclusively children**. Pe Kha Lau (71) shows the
population is better described as *people without exit dependent on a withdrawn sustaining
system* (the Tier-3 conduct-delineated test in `../../00-charge-theory.md`), with a heavy
pediatric weighting — not "children only." Recording this so the packet does not overclaim the
demographic.
