## Content (search-surfaced)

- Gawande — head of global health at USAID during the Biden administration — states "**hundreds
  of thousands of deaths have already occurred**" as a result of the loss of aid. *(realized,
  not projected — stated by the person who previously ran the programs)*
- He reported from **Kakuma refugee camp, Kenya**, on the impact of cuts on childhood
  malnutrition; documented in the short film *Rovina's Choice* (mother Rovina Naboi trying to
  save her daughter Jane from starvation).
- **The Lancet** independent analysis: USAID had **saved 92 million lives** over two decades,
  including **30 million children**, and estimated cuts could cause **14 million more deaths by
  2030**.
