## Honesty notes on the bundle

- **No CONTROL/NULL source is on record for DOGE.** This is a stated fact, not a hidden
  one (see `04-falsification-memo.md`). The absence is itself evidence-shaped but is
  declared, not relied on as proof.
- **Modeling is projection, not body count** — except where realized mortality is
  independently attested (`gawande-democracynow`, the named deaths). The case keeps the two
  registers distinct: projections establish **foreseeability and distribution**; named
  deaths and Gawande's statement establish **realized harm**. Do not let one borrow the
  other's authority.
- **`A1` (analyst inference)** appears only twice in the matrix (platform-amplification
  benefit; aggregate direction). Both are marked as inference. Nothing in the charge theory
  rests on an A1 item alone.
