# 04 — FALSIFICATION MEMO

*The instrument of intellectual honesty: the conditions under which this case would be
**wrong**, stated affirmatively and in advance, so that the record cannot be accused of
being unfalsifiable — the very property (Move 4, "unfalsifiable overlay") the framework
exists to name. A case that cannot say what would refute it is not a case; it is a creed.*

> Stating these conditions is not hedging. It is the difference between a record built to
> survive cross-examination and one built to feel true. If any condition below is met, the
> corresponding beam of the case is weakened or falls — and the packet must be revised, not
> defended.

---

## A. What would falsify the **instrument** claim (Track A / Art. II(3)(a))

The case would fail on the instrument element if:

1. **A methodology surfaced.** doge.gov/savings turns out to have had a published,
   auditable methodology and provenance for its savings claims, contemporaneous with the
   claims — making the "unauditable by design" finding false.
2. **The fabrications were corrected, not deleted.** The five deleted contracts, the
   $650M-listed-thrice entry, and the $232M→$560K and $8B→$8M errors were *publicly
   acknowledged and corrected with a logged error history*, rather than removed. (Correction
   is a CONTROL move; deletion is the laundering move. If the record shows correction, the
   Move-2/Move-4 finding weakens.)
3. **The metric actually moved.** An independent budget analysis as authoritative as Cato's
   shows a real, DOGE-attributable reduction in federal spending in 2025. (This would not
   excuse the mortality, but it would defeat the "stated goal not achieved on its own metric"
   diagnostic that currently helps close the gap formula.)

## B. What would falsify the **reduction / harm** claim (Art. IV(4))

1. **PEPFAR continuity.** Evidence that PEPFAR ART supply was in fact maintained through
   waivers/bridge funding such that treatment interruption did *not* occur at scale, and that
   the named deaths were misattributed (e.g., the clinics closed for unrelated reasons).
2. **The named deaths unravel.** The reporting on Babagana, Suza Kenyaba, Peter Lokoyen, or
   the Turkana children is retracted or shown to be causally unrelated to the funding
   suspension. (One retraction does not collapse the pattern; a *systematic* unravelling
   would.)
3. **The modeling is repudiated.** UNAIDS, the Lancet/UCLA analysis, and CGD substantially
   revise their projections downward by orders of magnitude on methodological grounds, and the
   realized-mortality statements (Gawande) are withdrawn.

## C. What would falsify the **foreseeability** claim (Art. II(3)(c) / Track C)

1. **Timeline collapse.** The peer-reviewed modeling, the Tamlyn cable, the ProPublica
   internal memos, or the February congressional letter are shown to **post-date** the
   relevant decisions, rather than being contemporaneous with the active dismantlement. The
   foreseeability case depends on the *dates*; if the warnings came after the irreversible
   decisions, legibility-at-deployment fails.
2. **The warnings did not reach the chain.** The Tamlyn cable and ProPublica memos are shown
   never to have entered the relevant decision-making channel at all — making the
   "inside-their-own-chain" finding false. (Note: the case already concedes it cannot show
   *personal reading*; this condition is the stronger claim that the channel itself is
   fictitious.)

## D. What would falsify the **authorization** claim (Track B)

1. **No directive role.** Documentary evidence that Musk and DOGE had *no* operational
   direction over the USAID dismantlement — that DOGE was advisory in fact as well as in
   formal description, and the withdrawal decisions were made and executed entirely elsewhere.
2. **The capture comes back empty.** The `musk-woodchipper-posts` and the "fired at the
   direction of DOGE" items, when preserved, turn out to be misattributed, fabricated, or
   materially out of context. (This is why these are the **priority captures** — the
   authorization track currently leans on them.)

## E. The CONTROL/NULL condition (what a counter-entry would require)

The master ledger records **no CONTROL or NULL entry on record** for DOGE. The case states
this as a fact and specifies what would create one — i.e., what a genuine piece of
exculpatory evidence would look like:

> A documented instance of DOGE identifying a **specific** instance of fraud or waste, with
> **verifiable methodology**, where the savings figure was **independently confirmed**, the
> program was **objectively redundant**, and **no accountability mechanism was removed** in the
> process — *and*, for this case specifically, where the program cut was **not** one whose
> withdrawal produces foreseeable mortality in a population without exit.

If such an instance is produced, it does not by itself falsify the case (one good cut does not
launder the PEPFAR withdrawal), but it would establish that DOGE is **capable** of the
non-laundering form, which would require the case to argue selection rather than essence — a
materially harder argument. The honest statement is: **the absence of any such instance to date
is part of what closes the gap formula; the appearance of one would reopen it.**

## F. The reflexivity condition (the case turning in the hand)

Per Art. V(3) and the Reflexivity Clause: if this packet itself were found to **corrupt
discernment** — to suppress exculpatory evidence, to misrepresent a source, to brand a good-faith
DOGE defender's rebuttal as itself a laundering move in order to avoid engaging it — then the
packet would fall **within its own terms**, and the relevant finding must be struck. The
protected-activity wall runs in both directions: it protects DOGE's defenders' honest rebuttals
absolutely, and it binds this packet to engage them rather than disqualify them.

## Standing instruction

This memo is to be **re-run against every future revision** of the packet. Any new item added
to the evidence matrix must be accompanied by the question: *what would falsify this?* An item
that cannot be falsified does not belong in a cold record — it belongs in the cathedral, where
the hot register lives. The knife stays cold by staying falsifiable.
