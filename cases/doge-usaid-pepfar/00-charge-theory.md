## The charge, in one sentence

**Elon Musk and DOGE deployed an administrative-justificatory instrument — the
"waste and fraud" framing and the doge.gov/savings apparatus — that rendered the
withdrawal of life-sustaining PEPFAR and USAID support undiscernable as harm, while the
foreseeable result of that withdrawal was the death, at scale and disproportionately of
children, of a population with no political standing in the system performing the
calculation.**

This is **instrumental veriticide by administrative withdrawal**: not the telling of a
lie, but the organized defeat of the shared capacity to recognize population-level harm
as harm while intervention remained possible.

## The spine (the prosecutor's order of proof)

> Prove the population · prove the reduction · prove the instrument · prove the pattern ·
> prove the authorization · prove the warnings · prove the dismissal · preserve the chain.

Each clause maps to a track in `01-evidence-matrix.md`. The case is not any single item;
the case is the direction all the items point together.

## The protected population (operationalized, not sloganized)

Not "the permanent underclass" as slogan. A **produced, conduct-delineated group**, defined
by relation to the conduct and read off its effects (Tier 3 test):

1. **Exposure to disruption** — dependency on a sustaining system (ART supply, RUTF food).
2. **Lack of exit** — no private means to replace withdrawn ART or therapeutic food.
3. **No political standing** — no vote, lobby, or legal recourse in the U.S. system doing
   the cost-benefit calculation.
4. **Recurring appearance as cost-bearing object** — the population that consistently
   bears the reduction across the output corpus.

Sharpest edge: **the pediatric ART and acute-malnutrition subset** — Babagana (7), Suza
Kenyaba (5), Peter Lokoyen (4), and at least 54 named children in Turkana, Kenya. The
group did not have to be named by the perpetrator to be the group; it is whoever the
instrument demonstrably laundered harm against.

## The instrument (Art. II(3)(a))

DOGE as a bureaucratic-justificatory apparatus, deployed over USAID operations from
January–February 2025:
- the **"waste and fraud"** frame (the reframing-as-care act, Art. II(2)(c) reverse
  variant: humanitarian function recoded as the *evidence of* waste);
- the **doge.gov/savings "wall of receipts"** — self-labeled a "subset," methodology-free,
  with contested entries deleted rather than corrected: **unauditable by design**;
- **amplification on X**, the deployer's own platform, with documented preferential reach.

The instrument satisfies Art. II(3)(a) not because it is an AI, but because it produced
**locally defensible justifications** (each canceled "contract" looks like ordinary
budgeting) at an **institutional scale and speed** that defeated contemporaneous
recognition of the aggregate direction.

## Tier placement (Stack Taxonomy)

- **Tier 3 — Subgraph Erasure**: a conduct-delineated population reduced while the
  reduction is rendered as that population's benefit ("for the taxpayers").
- **Tier 4 — Veriticide proper**: the discernment commons attacked — "no one has died"
  deployed against a closed documentary record; the evaluators removed.

Tiers 5–6 (mundicide / worldcide) and the AI-conscription layer are **left in the
cathedral**. This packet does not reach for them. The convergence is the map; this is one
road segment.

## Charging register note — why conscription is dropped here

The Convention Commentary and the project's adversarial review both hold that the
**base offense must be able to survive without the conscription element** for non-AI
administrative, propaganda, and institutional systems; conscription stays load-bearing
only for **aggravated AI veriticide**. DOGE is a bureaucratic instrument, not a model
built from uncompensated expression. Charging conscription here would force a
training-data discovery fight that has nothing to do with how these particular people
died, and hand the respondents an easy attack on a beam this case does not need. **Track E
is therefore marked N/A — not absent, just not the instrument-type at issue.** This is a
deliberate narrowing, recorded so no signatory leans on a beam that was never tested here.

## What this charge theory does NOT establish

- Criminal **intent** in the specific-intent sense (not required — and deliberately not
  claimed; the mental element is knowledge / engineered ignorance, Art. II(3)(d)).
- **Exclusive causal attribution** of any individual death to Musk's or DOGE's specific
  acts as opposed to the administration's aggregate decisions. The case is built on
  **pattern and foreseeable distribution** (Art. IV), not single-instance but-for causation.
- That the genocide characterization in the ledger's Genocide Supplement is the *first*
  thing to charge. It is the historical truth; it is archived in the cathedral; it is not
  the prosecutable wedge. Charge the **function** first.
