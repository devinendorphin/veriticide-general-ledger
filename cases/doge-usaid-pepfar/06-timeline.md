**contemporaneous with**, not subsequent to, the decisions and their continuation. The
foreseeability case (Art. II(3)(c)) and the mental element (Art. II(3)(d)) live in the

**WARN** warning/foreseeability · **CONT** continuation despite warning / suppression ·

## What the ordering establishes

1. **Warning precedes and accompanies continuation.** The Jan 24 letter, the Feb 6/10
   waiver-failure reporting, the Feb 27 modeling, the early-2025 internal memos, and the Apr 21
   Tamlyn cable all **predate** the May 21 denial — and most predate the bulk of the
   continuation (USIP takeover, payment vetoes sustained through spring). Legibility at the
   decision point (Art. II(3)(c)) is shown by sequence, not assertion.
2. **Harm is interleaved, not post-hoc.** Babagana (Feb 2) and Suza Kenyaba (Feb 19) die **before**
   the waivers could have functioned and months before the denial. The deaths are not a late
   projection; they are contemporaneous with the decisions.
3. **Continuation despite knowledge is the mental element.** Between the warnings (left) and the
   denial (bottom), the named respondent **continued and defended** the conduct — the box-by-box
   payment vetoes, the eviction of evaluators — which is where Art. II(3)(d) (knowledge /
   engineered ignorance) is carried.

## What this timeline does NOT establish

Exact-day precision for the rows marked ~ (confirm at original-form capture); personal receipt
of any internal warning by a specific named individual (the timeline shows the *channel and
sequence*, not the eyeball); and single-instance but-for causation (the case is built on
sequence + pattern + foreseeable distribution, per Art. IV).
