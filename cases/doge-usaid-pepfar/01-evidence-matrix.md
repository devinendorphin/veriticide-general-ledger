## What this matrix establishes, and does not

**Establishes:** a sourced, multi-track pattern in which (A) an unauditable justificatory
instrument (B) was deployed by a named director over USAID operations, (C) against a
contemporaneously legible mortality trajectory documented inside the actors' own chain,
(D) with the evaluators removed and the result denied to Congress.

**Does not establish:** specific-intent; exclusive but-for causation of any single death;
the conscription element (N/A); criminal guilt (reserved to a tribunal). It establishes the
**function** and the **foreseeable distribution** — which is what Art. IV is built to carry.
