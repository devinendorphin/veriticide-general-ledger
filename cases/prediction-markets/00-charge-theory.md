## The charge, in one sentence

**The prediction-market architecture — a reality-pricing engine that converts belief about future
events into an institutionally-deferred-to price — is operated and regulated so as to collapse the
distinction between a *forecast* outcome and a *caused* one, and to keep open a destruction-skewed,
single-actor-causable contract menu (war, assassination, regime change) by standing down the
protective wall after the outcome-channel harm is already on the record — thereby corrupting the
shared capacity to distinguish what the world *is* from what an interested party *priced and then
delivered*.**

This is **instrumental veriticide at the reality-pricing layer**: the Article II(2)(d) act —
rendering the harm undiscernible — performed not by a false statement but by an **architecture, a
self-sealing resolution rule, and a permissive regulatory posture**, by a network in which the
entity that prices reality and the entity that produces reality are merging.

## The spine (order of proof)

> Grant the virtue · prove the engine · prove the two channels · prove the bettability asymmetry ·
> prove the forecast/cause collapse · prove the foreseeable harm on the record · prove the wall stood
> down after that record · prove the convergence · preserve the chain.

## What this case charges — and the joint it deliberately sidesteps

The hardest joint here is **intent**: did anyone *deliberately* cause a war or atrocity to win a bet?
At current market depth that reading **overshoots**, and advancing it would hand the comfortable
reader permission to file the whole structure under paranoia. So this case **does not charge
deliberate-atrocity-for-profit.** It charges the **two affordable, already-operating mechanisms**:

1. **The perception channel** — moving the *displayed number* (cheap in thin markets) and letting
   institutional deference convert it into reality's official record ("the market knows" replaces
   "I checked").
2. **The outcome channel** — the insider who already holds the lever, for whom the marginal cost of
   betting on his own decision is ~zero and depth is irrelevant because the public pays for the
   outcome (`vandyke-venezuela-insider-bet-2026`).

This mirrors the bot-swarm packet's concession of **attribution** and the Rubio packet's concession
of general causation: the Tier 4 harm (defeat of the forecast/cause distinction) is cognizable *in
itself* without proving anyone intended an atrocity.

## The good-faith case, granted first (or the analysis is just disqualification reversed)

Prediction markets genuinely aggregate dispersed knowledge and on some questions match or beat
survey/expert forecasts (`fed-staff-forecast-accuracy`, held PENDING-PRIMARY). The price is **not**
meaningless. The thesis is that **the virtue and the vulnerability are one organ**: the price is
valuable *because* people believe it, which is exactly what turns moving the price into moving the
record. Refusing to grant the virtue would itself be the disqualification-of-dissent move pointed the
other way (Reflexivity Clause / Art. V).

## The protected interest (Art. II(1), second limb)

The **discernment commons at the reality-pricing layer** — society's shared capacity to recognize
what is true or about-to-be-true, as distinct from what an interested party priced and then
delivered. The **forecast/cause collapse** is the defining Tier 4 injury (Entry 17): the market is
always right in retrospect because it resolves to whatever happened, dissolving the category one
would need to tell manufacture from forecast. At the outcome channel the protected interest extends
to the **populations subject to single-actor-causable bettable harms** (the Venezuela operation as
the documented instance).

## The instrument (Art. II(3)(a)) — *a genuine system (like the bot-swarm recommender)*

The instrument is the **reality-pricing engine itself**: a continuously-quoted price that
institutions and publics consult as the authoritative reading of the world — a fluent justificatory
system operating at a scale and speed beyond unaided human agency, plus the permissive regulatory
rule that keeps the destruction-skewed menu open. The contested beam here is **not** the instrument;
it is **intent** (conceded).

## What this charge theory does NOT establish

- That any operator or official **intends** to cause wars/atrocities for payout (conceded; not charged).
- That any specific quoted **price caused** any election result or military decision.
- That **Donald Trump Jr. placed any trade** (a category error; the conflict is the merging of pricer
  and producer, not one man's ticket — `03-adversarial-check.md`, Defense 3).

  **granted** (`fed-staff-forecast-accuracy`); the protected-activity wall (Art. V) is load-bearing.
