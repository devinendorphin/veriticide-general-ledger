## A. The facts on the record

- A U.S. Army Special Forces master sergeant, **Gannon Ken Van Dyke, 38**, was arrested on a DOJ
  indictment accusing him of using classified information to profit on the Polymarket prediction
  market from the U.S. military mission ("Operation Absolute Resolve") that captured Venezuelan
  leader **Nicolás Maduro** (and his wife, Cilia Flores) in early January 2026.
- Van Dyke was **involved in the planning and execution** of the operation.
- He **wagered ~$33,000 across roughly 13 bets in the week before** the operation, taking "Yes"
  positions on contracts that U.S. forces would be in Venezuela by Jan. 31, 2026; that Maduro would
  be out of office by that date; that the U.S. would invade by Jan. 31; and that President Trump
  would invoke the War Powers Act against the country by that date.
- The bets **won him nearly $410,000** (~$410K).
- **Concealment:** after news outlets reported unusual trading in Maduro-related contracts, Van Dyke
  took steps to conceal his identity as the trader; on or about **January 6, 2026** he asked
  Polymarket to delete his account, falsely claiming he had lost access to the email address.
- **Charges:** unlawful use of confidential government information for personal gain; theft of
  nonpublic government information; commodities fraud; wire fraud; and making an unlawful monetary
  transaction.

## B. Why it is in this case

This is the **outcome channel made concrete** (Entry 17): for the insider who already holds the
lever, the marginal cost of betting on the decision he is making is ~zero, and market depth is
irrelevant because the public — not the bettor — pays for the outcome. It also anchors
**foreseeability** (Art. II(3)(c)): the harm pathway was on the public record *before* the regulator
withdrew the proposed ban on the destruction-skewed contract categories
(`cftc-withdraws-political-sports-ban-2026`). The case does **not** allege the bet *caused* the
operation; it documents that the architecture priced, and paid out on, a single-actor-causable
lethal state action.
