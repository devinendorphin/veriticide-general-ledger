## A. The facts on the record

- **Donald Trump Jr.** has been a **paid strategic advisor to Kalshi** since January 2026
  (reportedly granted ~**$300K in equity** for a senior-adviser role — single-outlet figure, flag).
- Via **1789 Capital** (the venture firm where he is a partner), Trump Jr. made a **double-digit-
  millions investment in Polymarket** and **joined Polymarket's advisory board**.
- He therefore advises the **two dominant platforms at once.** A spokesman: *"This doesn't change
  anything regarding Don's role with Kalshi"* and *"Don is committed to supporting the prediction
  market industry as a whole."*
- Coverage notes it is "not clear whether this rises to the level of a legal or regulatory conflict
  of interest" — the soothing framing this case's `03-adversarial-check.md` (Defense 3) refuses.

and a worked instance of **Triangulated Beneficence / register-splitting** (Pattern Registry Entry
