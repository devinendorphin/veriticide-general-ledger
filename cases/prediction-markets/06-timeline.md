## What the ordering establishes

1. **The wall was stood down *after* the harm, not before.** The Venezuela insider bet (Jan; charged
   Apr 23) and the Kalshi candidate enforcement (Apr 22) put the outcome-channel harm on the record
   *before* the June 10 permissive NPRM and during the same window as the withdrawal of the
   political/sports ban. A permissive posture maintained *after* the grave-risk record is the
   II(3)(c)/(d) inference.
2. **The exclusion wall is partial.** It fired on $100 candidate bets (Apr 22) but did **not** catch
   the $33K classified-insider war bet *ex ante* — a wall with the wrong threshold for the worst
   case.
3. **The convergence is contemporaneous.** Within ~weeks: operator (both platforms), competitor
   (Truth Predict), regulator (praised by the President), outcome-lever (the executive) all line up —
   and the coverage shows it was **declared in daylight**, not hidden.
4. **The benefit reframe is in the actors' own words.** "Thrive," "Gold Standard," "remain at the
   top" — financial-innovation framing applied to a menu that includes war and assassination
   contracts.
