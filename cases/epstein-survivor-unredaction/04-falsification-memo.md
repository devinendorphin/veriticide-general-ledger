# 04 — FALSIFICATION MEMO

*The conditions under which this case is wrong, stated in advance.*

> Re-run against every revision. The §Reproduction condition is as binding as the falsifiers.

---

## A. What would falsify the **asymmetry** (the load-bearing claim)

1. **No asymmetry.** A showing that possible enablers' identifying information was disclosed on
   comparable terms to victims' — i.e., the redaction failed symmetrically, not in a power-tracking
   direction. If exposure hit the powerful and the vulnerable alike, the inversion charge collapses
   to a generic (and likely protected) transparency-tradeoff complaint.
2. **Misread direction.** The "concealing possible enablers" reporting is shown to refer to lawful,
   narrow, time-limited investigative redactions unrelated to shielding the powerful.

## B. What would falsify the **exposure / harm**

1. **No exposure.** The "unredacted survivor info" reports are shown to be inaccurate — the material
   did not in fact identify survivors. (Multiple outlets + the survivors' own suit weigh heavily
   against this.)
2. **No realized harm.** The pleaded harms (harassment, threats, conspiratorial recoding) are shown
   to be unsubstantiated.

## C. What would falsify the **euphemism** charge (Move 5)

1. **Genuine isolated accident.** Evidence that the exposure was a single, narrow, promptly-cured
   slip — not thousands of documents across multiple releases after assurances — would make
   "technical error" an accurate description rather than a laundering of a foreseeable process
   failure.

## D. The intent question (explicitly NOT a falsifier of the case)

The case **does not assert intent.** Therefore "no proof of intent" does **not** falsify it — the
charge is the structure (asymmetry + euphemism + inversion) and constructive foreseeability. What
*would* matter: documentation that the redaction decisions were made with **due care** under a
reasonable process (which would bear on negligence), or, conversely, internal records showing the
asymmetry was directed (which would *escalate* beyond the current structural charge). The complaint
and any DOJ IG / oversight findings are the P1 to pull.

## E. The CONTROL condition

What a CONTROL would look like: the DOJ promptly and symmetrically curing the exposure, disclosing
enabler information on comparable terms, and correcting the record that the exposed individuals are
**victims, not conspirators** — with resourced victim-notification and protection. The documented
record (repeated exposure, asymmetry, "technical error," survivors left to sue) is the absence of
that CONTROL.

## F. The reproduction condition (the case turning into the harm) — binding

This packet charges the **exposure of survivors**. If the packet itself — now or in any future
revision — **reproduces, mirrors, links to, or reconstructs** any survivor's name, image, contact
information, or other identifying detail, or captures the primary leaked files into its evidence
store, it **commits the very act it charges** and the finding must be struck and the content purged.
The evidentiary base is **reporting that does not name survivors**, by design. Custody discipline
here is not neutral hygiene; it is the substance of the charge applied to ourselves.

## G. The reflexivity condition

The analyst is built by an AI lab; the survivors' suit names **Google** over the *proliferation* of
the exposed information, and search/AI systems are a general proliferation vector for exactly this
kind of exposed identity. Declared. The local discipline (no reproduction; capture reporting, not
files) is the minimal answer; the broader point — that the analyst's own category participates in the
proliferation harm pathway — is logged, not waved away.

---

## Standing instruction

Re-verify on every use: **asymmetry shown · intent not asserted · direction discriminator held
(expose the powerful = protected) · no survivor identity reproduced.** If the asymmetry is absent, it
is a transparency tradeoff, not this case. If a survivor is named, the packet has become the harm.
Both lines are the legitimacy.
