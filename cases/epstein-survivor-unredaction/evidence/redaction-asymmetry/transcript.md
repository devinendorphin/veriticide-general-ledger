## A. The asymmetry (the inversion tell)

- The DOJ release drew scrutiny for **"revealing victim info and concealing possible enablers"** —
  i.e., the redaction apparatus operated in **opposite directions for the two populations**:
  identifying information about **victims** was disclosed, while information about **possible
  enablers** of the trafficking was withheld/redacted.
- This is the structural core of the case: redaction's purpose is to **shield the vulnerable**;
  here it shielded the **powerful** (possible enablers) while exposing the **vulnerable** (victims).
  The direction of protection was inverted.

## B. Why it is the load-bearing item

The exposure alone (see `victim-info-exposed`) is consistent with negligence. The **asymmetry** is
what strains the "pure accident" account: an error that systematically protects the powerful and
exposes the vulnerable is, at minimum, an error whose *distribution* tracks power. The case does not
need to prove the asymmetry was intentional (see `04-falsification-memo.md`); the **documented
direction** of the inversion is the chargeable structure (Pattern Registry Entry 16). This is the
discriminator against the protected-activity defense (`03-adversarial-check.md`, Defense 1):
legitimate transparency exposes the powerful; this exposed the powerless and shielded the powerful.
