## A. The exposure

- Across the DOJ's "Epstein files" disclosures (late 2025–Feb 2026), **identifying information of
  numerous victims appeared unredacted** — reported to include names, email addresses, phone
  numbers, and images — and, per reporting, **several women whose identities had never before been
  publicly associated with the case** were named.
- The exposed material was reported to include **images of potential victims** alongside identifying
  text that was "either unredacted or not fully obscured."
- Lawyers for survivors stated the latest release **included survivors' names despite DOJ
  assurances** to the contrary.

## B. Why it is in this case

This is the predicate act: the apparatus that is supposed to **protect** the vulnerable instead
**exposed** them. It is the "expose-the-victims" half of the redaction-inversion charge
(`00-charge-theory.md`); the "shield-the-enablers" half is in `redaction-asymmetry`, and the
laundering ("technical error") is in `technical-error-framing`.
