## Honesty notes

- **The packet does not hold the primary leaked documents — by design.** Capturing the exposed
  files would replicate the harm. The evidentiary base is reporting that does not name survivors.
- **Intent is open.** No source establishes the exposure was deliberate; the case charges the
  structure (asymmetry + euphemism + inversion), not intent.
- **Legitimate disclosure of enablers is the *missing* good, not a target.** The asymmetry is that
  the powerful were *under*-disclosed; this packet's critique runs toward more transparency for
  enablers and less exposure of victims — never the reverse.
