## What this matrix establishes, and does not

**Establishes:** that a state disclosure apparatus exposed a vulnerable protected population while
concealing the powerful, laundered it as error, and that the exposure produced realized harm and the
recoding of victims as conspirators — the Redaction-Inversion structure with constructive
foreseeability (II(3)(c)) by a system-scale instrument (II(3)(a)).

**Does not establish:** that the exposure was intentional (intent open — the hardest joint); the
specific officials responsible; or any survivor identity (deliberately never reproduced). Custody is
LOCATOR→VERIFIED on the *reporting*, not on the underlying files (which the packet refuses to mirror).
