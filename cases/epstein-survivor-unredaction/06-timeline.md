## What the ordering establishes

1. **The exposure followed assurances.** Protection was promised, then breached, repeatedly, at the
   scale of thousands of documents — the foreseeability that the "error" framing conceals.
2. **The error had a direction.** The same release that **exposed victims** **concealed possible
   enablers** — an accident that systematically protects the powerful is, at minimum, a negligence
   whose distribution is the finding.
3. **The harm was the recognition, not only the data.** Beyond exposure, survivors were **recoded as
   conspirators** — the victim→perpetrator inversion that is the Tier-4 signature.
